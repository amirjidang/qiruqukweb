Rapper Bhad Bhabie Claims She Earned $1 Million In Six Hours, Breaking Only Fans Record

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  463 words  |  Tandra Barner

Rapper Bhad Bhabie claims to have just broken a record by making over $1 million in 6 hours on her OnlyFans account, and fans are stunned to see the turnaround on this huge payday. By charging a subscription fee of $23.99, she has secured a very healthy chunk of change that gets padded to the wealth she already generates through music sales. Pursuing an OnlyFans account has been a really lucrative move for Bhad Bhabie, and as Page Six reports, the breakdown of how this money was earned so quickly, will surely shock many.

Bhad Bhabie has a whopping 17.1 million followers on Instagram. Those that really want the inside scoop are able to subscribe to her OnlyFans account and that unlocks a lot of exclusive information.  Top images, details, videos, and information that is not widely shared is available for those fans that take the leap and invest in the OnlyFans subscription.

Clearly, this has proven to be a highly valued feature, and fans were quick to invest their money, leading to this huge payday for the 18 year old rapper.

In addition to all of this exclusive information, Bhad Bhabie gave fans access to message her directly, and that is perhaps what enticed fans to pay for this access. In lieu of collecting these big bucks from her fans, Bhad Bhabie promises to make herself available and send personal responses to excited followers.

Bhad Bhabie was first introduced to the public with her real name, Danielle Bregoli.  She appeared on the Dr. Phil show in 2016, as a wildly rude and aggressive teenager with parents that were distraught and defeated by her behavior.

Her tougher-than-nails attitude persisted on the show, as she challenged people to a fight by asking them to "catch her outside" which eventually saw her coined as the "cash me outside girl."

Capitalizing on her 5 minutes of fame, Danielle went on to create music and has since made a real name for herself in the music scene... never really shedding that attitude that got her noticed in the first place.

She is now  promising to keep in touch with fans through direct messaging if they continue to pay her these big bucks.

Clearly, this promise has worked.

Boasting $757,526.08 from subscription payments, $267,675 from payments that have been generated from direct messaging through OnlyFans, and roughly $5,502.35 in tips that this platform has rounded up for her, OnlyFans has proven to be a fabulous forum for the young star to increase her net worth.

In less than 6 hours, she was able to rake in over $1 million, and that's just the beginning. This enormous income is expected to continue to rise.
