What Happened To Vanessa Angel?

Vanessa Angel was one of the most loved actresses in the Indonesian entertainment industry as she has starred in various hit projects.

She had been a victim of several controversies in the past days. In January 2019, she was caught in an online prostitution case.

Angel was arrested in a hotel room in Surabaya and a businessman who ordered her prostitution services.

And the news of her arrest made headlines on the internet and grabbed the eyes of many people. Model Avriellia Shaqqila was also arrested.

East Java police chief Arman Asmara said in-depth investigations through social media recommended that Shaqqila was charging 25 million rupiahs (US$1,750) and Angel was charging 80 million rupiahs (US$5,600) for sexual services.

Vanessa Angel Mobil Menabrak And Kecelakaan Details

Vanessa Angel died on November 4, 2021, due to a car accident on the Nganjuk-Surabaya Toll Road, the Jombang section, KM 672+400A.

The incident occurred at around 12:36 West Indonesia Time. At that time, Angel was in a white Mitsubishi Pajero Sport with four other people, including her husband, young child, a domestic helper, and a personal driver.

The car was driven by its driver towards the city ​​of Surabaya. Sadly, Angel’s husband was also killed there. Besides, the household assistant suffered serious injuries, their child and the private driver suffered minor injuries.

Obituary: Vanessa Angel And Husband Bibi Adriyansh Cause Of Death

Vanessa Angel and her husband Bibi Adriyansh died on-site, while the three other victims were taken to the nearest hospital after reportedly sustaining serious injuries.

The fatal incident was confirmed by AKBP Dwi Sumrahadi, head of the East Java Police Highway Patrol unit, on Thursday, November 4, 2021, at around 14:00 Western Indonesia Time.

The body of Angel and her husband were taken to the Bhayangkara Hospital in Surabaya.

The couple was also blessed with a child named Gala Sky Andriansyah, who suffered a minor injury in that accident.
