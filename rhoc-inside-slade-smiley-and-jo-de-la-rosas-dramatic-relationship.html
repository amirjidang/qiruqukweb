Jo And Slade

Fans have been keeping up with the cast of RHOC, which includes Jo De La Rosa.

Jo was on The Real Housewives Of Orange County for seasons 1 and 2 and, according to Us Weekly, she also had a spin-off show called Date My Ex: Jo & Slade. Jo and Mark Lovette got engaged back in 2015 and the publication noted that since Jo got rid of any Instagram pictures of the two of them, it doesn't seem like they got married.

It seems like it would have been tough for Jo to watch Slade and Gretchen begin their relationship, as Jo and Gretchen were pals. Jo talked to Andy Cohen about the relationship and said, "Considering she had met Slade through me while we were dating, it was just kind of interesting to see that once I moved to L.A., they ended up together. But I'm happy for the both of them because I truly feel the two of them deserve each other."

Fans of RHOC will remember that Jo and Slade's relationship was really never in a great place. He thought that it would be a good idea if she stayed home and was a housewife, and she wanted to explore some career options. They weren't on the same page and many of their episodes featured this tension. Fans could tell that they would probably break up and that Jo needed someone would could offer her support since she had big career dreams.

Jo explained to Entertainment Weekly that when she thought about joining RHOC with Slade, "I kind of [wanted] to look at it as an amazing experience we could do together as a couple, having no idea what it would become." Jo also said, "I didn't even know who I was at 23. I was literally growing up in front of America."
