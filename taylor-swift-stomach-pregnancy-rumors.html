Nonetheless, Swift is way too busy at the moment to care. She recently gave an emotional speech during her second Eras Tour show in Lisbon.

She told fans, "We are currently in the Red era. And during the course of those tours... we made so many fun memories, but the one thing that I wish I would have done differently is I wish I could have brought every one of those tours to come see you in Lisbon."

Swift continued, “I have to say. I’ve just never seen a crowd like this in my life … There have been moments in this show already where I like, forget what I’m supposed to do next or what I’m supposed to say because I’m just so distracted by how much fun you’re having and how you’re performing in the audience. I love this.”

Travis Kelce is also keeping busy, recently attending the Mavericks-Timberwolves Game 3. Just prior, he was in Dallas attending an NHL Playoff game.

Taylor Swift Admitted In The Past That Bloated Pictures Of Herself Are A Bad Trigger

Credit to Swift as she has opened up in the past about her struggle overcoming an eating disorder. Among the triggers included seeing a bloated photo of herself.

Swift said, “It’s not good for me to see pictures of myself every day. It’s only happened a few times, and I’m not in any way proud of it. A picture of me where I feel like I looked like my tummy was too big, or… someone said that I looked pregnant … and that’ll just trigger me to just starve a little bit — just stop eating.”

Swift went on to admit that she avoided the topic in the past, but is now comfortable speaking about it.

“I think I’ve never really wanted to talk about that before, and I’m pretty uncomfortable talking about it now,” she says quietly. “But in the context of every other thing that I was doing or not doing in my life, I think it makes sense."

With the Eras tour taking over Europe and the world, it only makes sense that Swift is prone to some bloating, especially when it comes to flying on her jet from place to place.
