Matthew Eappen’s parents are Deborah and Sunil Eappen, who founded the Matty Eappen Foundation after the tragic incident. Find out more about them.

Matthew Eappen was an infant who passed away on February 9, 1997, due to a fractured skull and subdural hematoma. He was just 8 months old when he caved to internal bleeding caused by the Shaken Baby Syndrome. 

His death drew people’s attention worldwide, and after that incident, his then-nanny Louise Woodward was arrested for his murder. Additionally, Eappen had eye injuries too.

Furthermore, a documentary is made which is produced by Channel 4 titled Louise Woodward: Villain or Victim? It shows the notorious case of Matthew’s demise and the trail ushering to the conviction of his set au pair, Louise Woodward.

Matthew Eappen Parents Sunil Eappen and Deborah In 2022

Matthew Eappen’s parents, Sunil Eappen and Deborah, are engaged in their own works in 2022. After the tragic incident of Matthew, it was reported that they founded the Matty Eappen Foundation.

The respective foundation was set up at the Boston Children Hospital. There Matthew had also spent the last few days of his very short life. Meanwhile, Matty Eappen Foundation aims to flare cognition about child abuse.

Moreover, Sunil and Deborah have continued their medical practice. Not to mention, Sunil is an anesthesiologist based in Boston, Massachusetts, while Deborah works as an ophthalmologist in Wellesley, Massachusetts.

Take A Look At Matthew Eappen Family

The death of Matthew deeply saddened the family of Matthew Eappen. His father, Sunil, wedded Deborah in 1990 after the pair met in medical school.

They had their first son Brendan in 1994, and they arranged to take on an au-pair. Besides, Deborah was working as a mother, and when her first child was born, she resumed her part-time job as an ophthalmologist.

And now, both Sunil and Deborah are still practicing medicine. After their son’s death, they opened a foundation in the memory of him that works for child welfare.

What Happened To Matthew Eappen?

Matthew Eappen was 8 months old at the time of his passing. Louise Woodward was the hired au pair to Eappen. She hails from England, came to America to make a living. 

She received an injunction from the Eappens to improve herself in late January 1997. And on February 4, 1997, Louise contacted the police to convey that Baby Matthew was encountering problems in breathing.
