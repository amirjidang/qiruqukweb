Madison Beer has an incredibly large online presence, so it should come as no surprise that there is an immense interest in her career and her personal life. Fans love the information about what she has been up to, and her social media pages are filled with great selfies and other images which offer a glimpse into the life of Beer and who she is as a person.

This includes her relationship history, and she has been linked to several men. One of her first high-profile relationships was with another singer, Jack Gilinsky. At the time, she was just a teenager, and although things did not end well, the former couple had been together for almost two years before their breakup. As for what went wrong, we cannot be sure, but we do know that they have both moved on. This is how…

Inside Madison Beer And Jack Gilinsky's Relationship?

Madison Beer has had several high-profile relationships, and there is no denying there is an interest in her life love. Her past relationships include Brooklyn Beckham and Zack Bia, but arguably, her most memorable coupling was with Jack Gilinsky.

Jack Gilinsky is a singer born on Sept. 10, 1996, in Omaha, Nebraska, making him almost three years older than Madison Beer, who was born on March 5, 1999, in Jericho, New York. According to Cosmopolitan, their relationship started in 2015 after they met at a party through a mutual friend.

Beer has reflected upon how they met during an interview with W magazine, telling the publication that Nash Grier introduced them. "And we actually went on a vacation together with two other people, and there was no tension, nothing weird, no kissing, nothing — it was just like friends chilling, hanging out, and I had a little bit of a crush on him," she explained.

"I remember my dad called me, and we were fighting, and this guy who was there was like, 'This is why I would never date a 15-year-old.' And I was so mad because I thought, what if Jack heard that and doesn't want to date me anymore? Then fast-forward probably like a month or two later, we told each other we liked each other a lot, and it was really cute! When he told me that, he was on tour, and we officially started dating when he got home, so we've been together for like a year now."

They would date for almost two years, finally splitting in 2017. Their breakup was not without controversy either, and Jack Gilinsky reportedly had a recording of him leaked in which he had been verbally abusive to Madison Beer.

According to People Magazine, around the time of the recording, Beer took to social media to share a message (which was later deleted) saying, ​​"Don't make the same mistakes I did, your safety is never worth it. No matter what, no one deserves to be treated that way…"

But was this the end of it?

What Has Madison Beer Said About Her Former Relationship?

It seems that Madison Beer and Jack Gilinsky still had unresolved feelings for each other two years later (at least temporarily). According to Hollywood Life, in 2019, they were spotted on a lunch date, and Gilinsky is reported to have leaned over to kiss Beer on her lips. An action that the publication claims was not a "friendly peck" between friends either.

But both she and Gilinsky have since moved on. Beer is dating Nick Austin (and has been for almost two years since first being linked in 2020), and in 2023, she gave an interview about their relationship with InStyle.

"I'm super happy within my relationship, and I feel really safe, and I feel like I'm with someone I consider my best friend, which is very important," she said. "In the past, I haven't really spent 'friend time' with my boyfriend, whereas now, I kind of do. So, it feels good."

In 2022, it was revealed that Jack Gilinsky would become a father. The baby mama is Geneva Natalia, and the couple shared their exciting news on social media in August 2022 before they welcomed their daughter a month later.

Who Is Jack Gilinsky's Current Partner And Baby Mama Geneva Natalia?

Jack Gilinsky is dating model Geneva Natalia, who according to J-14, is represented by Two Management. The timeline for when they got together is unclear, but they made their relationship Instagram-official in 2022.

Since then, they have shared multiple images of each other and tributes to their relationship. This includes Gilinsky posting photos of himself and a pregnant Natalia looking very loved up. He captioned the post telling fans: "i wrote & recorded a song with baby mama on my lap (sic)."

He has also shared several photos of them together as a family, along with their daughter, whom he adores.

If you have been wondering if there was any overlap between Madison Beer and Geneva Natalia, who could have possibly known each other, it seems incredibly unlikely. The former couple broke up several years before Gilinsky and Natalia were ever linked, and it does not appear that either of them was still hung up on their past relationship.

The outcome is positive for all involved parties. Gilinsky has started a family and appears to be incredibly happy, and Beer is happy and comfortable in her own relationship with Nick Austin. No need for speculation here! They both continue to have massive fan interest in them and appear to be doing well for themselves.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Beer's estimated net worth is $18 million (already $2 million more than estimated less than two years ago). As for Jack Gilinsky? While Celebrity Net Worth does not estimate his earnings to be anywhere as high as his ex-girlfriend, he is believed to have an impressive $2 million.
