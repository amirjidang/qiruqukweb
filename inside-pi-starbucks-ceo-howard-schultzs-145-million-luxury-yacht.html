Howard Schultz’s $145 Million yacht is the definition of elegance and luxury.

Howard Schultz is the CEO of the international coffee house chain Starbucks. Starting from just one coffee bean shop in Seattle, Starbucks is currently present across 77 countries with more than 30000 stores. Due to this behemoth business, Howard Schultz has accumulated a massive net worth of around $4 Billion. He bought the super luxury yacht Pi in 2019 for a staggering $145 Million.

Yachts might be tagged as extravagant, ground-breaking, or spectacular. Still, Starbucks CEO Howard Schultz's $145 Million super yacht, Pi, the epitome of elegance and luxury, has also been given the title of ‘Auspicious.’

The Origins Of Howard’s Dream Vessel

The Pi Yacht was built in 2019 by a Dutch shipyard known as Feadship. With a length of around 77.8 meters, Pi was the 22nd largest boat built by Feadship, as reported by Superyacht Times. She is a magnificent piece of art with an aluminum superstructure and steel hull. The Pi boasts a unique design created using state-of-the-art glass technology from a computer-aided 3D design.

She was formerly known as Syzygy 818, a lucky name per Chinese numerology. She bears the former owner's name, whose birthday is August 18. Consequently, the superyacht's official name also included the number. The second part of her name, Syzygy, is an astrological term that describes the rare occurrence when three celestial bodies line up precisely in the sky.

Inside The $145 Million Luxury Vessel

With extraordinary build quality, outstanding design, and innovation, the PI is on the verge of perfection. The yacht's stunning Zen-inspired interiors were built by Sinot Exclusive Yacht Design, and De Voogt Naval Architects looked after the naval design as per Yacht Bible. One of the best features of Pi is the placement of floor-to-ceiling windows without mullions, which realizes the owner's idea of the main saloon, which genuinely complements the exterior.

Her elegant and welcoming atmosphere is accentuated by ageless design, magnificent furniture, and comfortable seating. At the 2019 World Superyacht Honors, Pi won awards for Motor Yacht of the Year and Best Displacement Motor Yacht Between 1,500GT and 1,999GT, according to Luxury launches. The finishing touch that provided Howard Schultz with a winner to sail was the flawless construction quality of the Feadship.

The yacht can accommodate up to 12 guests in 16 opulent guest rooms with a staff of 18 people. She has a luxurious spa and a pool with a glass bottom, among other features. The comfortable and stylish Pi features several distinctive features, including a spectacular spiral staircase connecting three floors and a large sitting area with a sofa accommodating eight people. She also boasts of a helipad and a garage.

Pi’s Flamboyant Exterior

With a length of 77.8 meters and a width of 11 meters, Pi is the 195th largest yacht in the world, as per News Rebeat. An aluminum superstructure supports the Pi's teak decks and steel hull, and she weighs around 1,592 tons. She comfortably sails at 16 knots and can attain a top speed of 18 knots thanks to dual diesel MTU 16-cylinder 2,700hp engines operating at 1800rpm. At-anchor stabilizers on Pi offer outstanding levels of comfort. Her freshwater tanks have a capacity of about 33,168 liters. She was constructed under the standards of the Lloyds Register classification society.

The vessel's external lines stand out for their inventive use of structural glass with a twofold curvature. The ship, which Jarko Jammsen created as his first superyacht project, has four decks ideally suited for guests' relaxation and entertainment. The big main deck aft can be set up for a casual setting, outdoor dining, or a lot of sunbathing. Additionally, a large upper deck behind the ship connects to the flush foredeck through side walkways. The best location on board for viewing the surroundings is the sundeck above.

Howard Schultz has got himself a yacht as exquisite as his coffee beans. He has transformed Starbucks and his life sailing in style with his $145 Million Pi. With her classy interiors, flamboyant exterior, and state-of-the-art technology, Pi is certainly a game-changer in the category of luxury Yachts.
