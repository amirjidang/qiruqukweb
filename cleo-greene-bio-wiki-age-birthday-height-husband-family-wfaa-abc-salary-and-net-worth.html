Cleo Greene Career

Cleo Greene WFAA Dallas

Currently, Greene is working as a weekend morning anchor as well as a reporter at WFAA located in Dallas. She joined the network in January 2018. Before that, she worked as a reporter at KSTP-TV Channel 5 located in Greater Minneapolis St Paul Area from February 2016 until January 2018. From October until February 2016, she served as a reporter at WBOC-TV located in Salisbury, Maryland.

Before that, Greene worked as a reporter at WRDW News 12 NBC 26 located in Augusta, Georgia, the United States from February 2011 until October 2011. Prior, to that, she served as a feature reporter/anchor PBS located in St Croix, US Virgin Islands from January 2009 until February 2011.
