5 Celebs Who Adore Their Fans (&amp; 5 Who Aren't Very Nice)

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  961 words  |  Martina Birk

Celebs who love their fans know what they are doing in the Hollywood industry. mMaintaining tight relationships with fans is so important for continual and long-lasting success. Celebs who interact with their fans on social media, during performances, at events, and at meet-and-greets are totally winning.

There is another side to the story too though... celebs who hate being around their fans! Plenty of celebs don't have the patience to deal with adoring fans-- even though fan bases are usually the reason why celebs are as wealthy and successful as they are.

10 Loves Her Fans: Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is an example of a celebrity who absolutely loves her fans. She appreciates the love that her fans give her all the time and she proved that when she decided to provide a group of her fans with free hot chocolate and cookies while they waited for her Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show performance. She definitely did not have to do that but she went above and beyond.

9 Doesn’t Care For His Fans: Christian Bale

Christian Bale has a bad reputation for the way he acts with his fans. He has reportedly made fans cry from his brash and harsh responses. He does not care to take pictures with fans and would rather not be bothered by them at all. It seems like several fans of his who approached him for pictures walked away with bad tastes in their mouths from their interactions with him.

8 Loves His Fans: Zac Efron

Zac Efron totally loves his fans! He has a massive amount of respect and love for them. On the set of Baywatch, a fan excitedly tried to take a selfie with Zac Efron but he accidentally dropped his iPhone. The screen shattered! Zac Efron still posed for a picture with the fan and then bought the fan a brand new iPhone! It was super considerate of him. Another example of a celeb going above and beyond.

7 Doesn’t Care For Her Fans: Kate Winslet

Kate Winslet is known for totally and utterly ignoring her fans. She doesn’t make eye contact or give them the time of day. Honestly, it sounds pretty rude! Kate Winslet is an award-winning actress who has taken home awards including the Academy Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role and Golden Globe Award for Best Actress Motion Picture Drama.

RELATED: Harry Potter: Kate Winslet Nearly Played This Character

She also won the BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role. It would be great if she gave her fans a little bit of love in return.

6 Loves Her Fans: Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus totally loves her fans! She has such a loving and caring energy about her, it’s not surprising at all that she acts in a loving way towards her fans as well. She invited a homeless fan to be her date to the MTV Music Video Awards one year and when she was invited to another fan's prom, she invited the student to join her on stage since she wouldn’t be able to attend his prom with him.

5 Doesn’t Care For His Fans: Robert Pattinson

Robert Pattinson has described his Twilight fans as being weird. Just to be clear, he has not spoken poorly about his fans from other movies that he has starred in... But he isn’t too proud of the work he did in the Twilight movie franchise and thinks that fans of the franchise are fairly weird people. Twilight movies really put him on the map though... it is true that the franchise is pretty cringe-worthy and creepy, especially now looking back on it.

4 Loves His Fans: Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber loves his fans! He stopped doing meet and greets a few years ago because they were emotionally draining him and causing him too much stress but that doesn’t mean that he doesn’t totally still care about his fans.

RELATED: Justin Bieber Uplifts Fans With His Positive Messaging

He has done a lot of things from buying new phones for his fans to donating money to even planning a surprise quinceanera for a teenage girl. He even purchased her quinceanera gown! Talk about generosity.

3 Doesn’t Care For Her Fans: Cameron Diaz

Cameron Diaz is known for not being a huge fan of her fans. When her fans approach her for pictures and autographs, she constantly refuses all of the above. She made the claim that if she did it for one fan, she would have to do it for all her fans and that simply isn’t in her best interest. At one premiere evemt, she even lectured her fans about how dumb autographs are.

2 Loves Her Fans: Lady Gaga

Lady Gaga totally loves her fan base. Her fans are known as “Little Monsters”. She’s always very responsive on Instagram and Twitter when her fans send her loving messages. She publicly responds and lets her fans know that she is hearing them for what they have to say! When she performs, she invites her fans on stage with her pretty often as well! She is very thoughtful when it comes to her fan base.

1 Doesn’t Care For His Fans: Adam Levine

Adam Levine is known for not having any patience for his fans or much desire to interact with his fans. Although his fans adore him a lot and would love to get autographs and pictures with him, he doesn’t care to take the time out of his day to do those things. It is reported that he decides which fans are worthy of autographs based on their appearances. Fans have called him a jerk in the past for his behavior.
