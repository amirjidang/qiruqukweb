How Shay Mitchell's And Ashley Benson's Net Worths Compare Since 'Pretty Little Liars' Ended

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  887 words  |  Billy Koelling

In the summer of 2010 the teen mystery thriller drama Pretty Little Liars premiered and it quickly became a huge hit. The show ended up running for seven seasons and it even resulted in a couple of spinoffs. Shay Mitchell and Ashley Benson ended up becoming two of the show's biggest stars and fans still keep up with them even though the show wrapped up four years ago.

Today, we're taking a look at just how the two actresses' wealth compares post-Pretty Little Liars. In case you ever wondered whether Shay Mitchell or Ashley Benson has a higher net worth — keep on scrolling to find out!

10 Both Ladies Rose To Fame On The Teen Mystery Drama 'Pretty Little Liars'

We're kicking the list off with the fact that while both Ashley Benson and Shay Mitchell had some experience in the industry prior to Pretty Little Liars, it was certainly the show that helped them achieve international fame. In the ABC Family/Freeform teen drama, Ashley Benson played Hanna Marin while Shay Mitchell portrayed Emily Fields. The show premiered in 2010, and after seven seasons it wrapped up in 2017.

9 Since The Show Wrapped Up, Benson Appeared In Two Movies

Since 2017, fans got to see Ashley Benson in two movies. She portrayed Roxie Rotten in the 2018 drama movie Her Smell which currently has a 5.8 rating on IMDb. This year, fans got to see her as Tracey in the crime thriller movie The Birthday Cake which has a 4.3 rating on IMDb. Currently, Ashley Benson has one upcoming project — the short movie Ask Me If I Care.

8 While Mitchell Appeared In Only One

While Ashley Benson wasn't too busy with movie roles since Pretty Little Liars wrapped up — neither was Shay Mitchell.

In fact, the actress appeared in only one movie since 2017, the supernatural horror thriller The Possession of Hannah Grace. The movie was released in 2018 and it currently has a 5.2 rating on IMDb. In it, Shay Mitchell played Megan Reed.

7 However, Benson Hasn't Appeared In Any Shows

Considering that the two ladies haven't appeared in too many movies since Pretty Little Liars ended, one would think that they worked on multiple shows instead. However, Ashley Benson seems to have taken things rather slowly as the actress hasn't appeared in one television project since the teen drama wrapped up in 2017.

6 While Mitchell Worked On Three

After Pretty Little Liars wrapped up, Shay Mitchell appeared in two very famous shows. In 2018 fans could see the actress portray Peach Salinger in the first season of the psychological thriller show You and since 2019 she's been playing Stella Cole in the comedy show Dollface. Besides these two, Mitchell also appeared as herself in an episode of RuPaul's Drag Race: All Stars and Almost Ready. This year, fans could hear the actress's voice in the anime-influenced shoe Trese in which she is the voice behind Alexandra Trese.

5 Both Actresses Are No Strangers To Brand Endorsements

Another source of income for both actresses are surely brand deals. Over the years they collaborated with plenty of brands to promote their products. Mitchell worked with brands like Pantene, American Eagle Outfitters, Acuvue, and Bioré while Ashley Benson recently collaborated with the brand Hempz.

4 And They Also Ventured Into Owning Businesses

Besides working with famous brands, both ladies ventured into starting their own businesses too. Shay Mitchell founded the production company Amore & Vita Inc., the travel brand BÉIS, as well as the tequila seltzer brand Onda.

Ashley Benson launched an eyewear capsule collection with Privé Revaux, as well as a fashion app called Daily Vice.

3 Shay Mitchell Has A Bigger Social Media Following

While both ladies managed to remain famous after Pretty Little Liars wrapped up, it seems as if Shay Mitchell is slightly more popular. Currently, Mitchell has 31.2 million followers on Instagram while Benson has 21.9 million. However, on Twitter, Mitchell currently has 3 million followers while Benson has 3.9 million. On TikTok, Mitchell has 6.4 million followers while Benson only has about 600,000 followers.

2 Ashley Benson Currently Has A Net Worth Of $6 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Ashley Benson is currently estimated to have a net worth of $6 million. A majority of the star's income comes from acting but some part of it also comes from social media and brand endorsements. However, it's safe to say that most of Ashley Benson's net worth comes from being on Pretty Little Liars for seven years.

1 And So Does Shay Mitchell

While it may seem as if Shay Mitchels is slightly more successful today, according to Celebrity Net Worth, the actress is also estimated to have a net worth of $6 million. Mitchell has a couple of very successful businesses so her net worth is bound to grow in the future, but for now, it seems as if the majority of her wealth still comes from Pretty Little Liars.
