Throughout the episode, Chris shares valuable insights and lessons learned from his elk hunting experience. He highlights the importance of gear selection and provides firsthand accounts of what worked and what didn’t during his time in the field. From bow choice to clothing, Chris leaves no stone unturned when discussing the impact of his gear decisions on the success of his hunting endeavors.

Aware of the significance of storytelling, Chris masterfully weaves his personal anecdotes and experiences with expert advice, resulting in an episode that is both entertaining and informative. Listeners will gain a deeper understanding of elk hunting strategies and techniques, all while being thoroughly engrossed in Chris’s engaging storytelling style.

    Podcast Recommendations

  • Whitetail Rendezvous

    Hosted by Bruce Hutcheon, Whitetail Rendezvous is a highly recommended podcast for whitetail enthusiasts. Bruce interviews some of the leading experts and personalities in the hunting industry, covering a wide range of topics related to whitetail hunting. From habitat management to bowhunting tactics, this podcast offers valuable insights and practical advice for all levels of hunters.

  • The Hunting Beast

    Dan Infalt, known as “The Beast” in the hunting community, hosts The Hunting Beast podcast. With a focus on mature buck hunting, Dan shares his knowledge and experiences as a successful whitetail hunter. His unconventional tactics and in-depth analysis of deer behavior make this podcast a favorite among serious whitetail hunters.

  • Wired to Hunt

    Hosted by Mark Kenyon, Wired to Hunt is a popular podcast that covers a wide range of hunting topics, with a strong focus on whitetails. Mark and his guests discuss everything from scouting techniques and hunting strategies to gear reviews and habitat management. The podcast provides valuable information and inspiration for hunters of all skill levels.
