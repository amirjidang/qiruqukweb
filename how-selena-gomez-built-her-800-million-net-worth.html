Here's How Selena Gomez Built Her $800 Million Net Worth

Highlights

  • Selena Gomez's net worth has reached an impressive $800 million due to her singing, acting, producing, and entrepreneurial ventures.
  • Her music career, including three number one albums and numerous top ten singles, has been a key factor in her financial success.
  • Gomez's strategic investments, producing credits, and endorsements from major brands have further bolstered her impressive net worth and solidified her position as a prominent figure in the entertainment industry.

Selena Gomez's net worth has risen to an astounding $800 million in 2023, making her one of the most prominent women in the entertainment industry today.

Her astonishing financial rise is due to her singing and performing abilities, as well as her diverse portfolio. Gomez has deliberately constructed an empire from her initial days on Disney Channel through her equally impressive music career, notable endorsements, and entrepreneurial initiatives like Rare Beauty.

Her investments in mental health projects along with significant social media earnings bolster her financial position. We'll go over the nine factors that have had the biggest impact on Selena Gomez's net worth and extensive career achievements.

Early Acting Career

Gomez's childhood acting career has helped pave the way for her successful venture into the entertainment industry. Her first screen appearance was at age ten, when she became one of the characters in the children's show, Barney & Friends as the character Gianna.

Her exposure (at a tender age) to acting was not without benefit, and it also gave her a preview of the life of a show business personality. Although she had appeared on television before, her roles in Disney Channel productions made her famous.

However, she became a household name after starring in Wizards of Waverly Place, a popular show from 2007 to 2012.

Music Success

Her music career has been a significant factor in building Selena Gomez's $800 million net worth. She first became known for her music when she was featured as the lead vocalist of Selena Gomez & The Scene, before striking out as a successful solo artist.

She is a music icon boasting a discography with three number one albums and nine top ten singles. Her first two solo studio albums, Stars Dance and Revival debuted at number one on the Billboard 200.

Gomez has made significant musical strides and generated substantial income through album sales, streaming and concert tours.

Film and Television Roles

Selena Gomez's participation in both film and on television has contributed significantly to her wealth. First, she became famous after Wizards of Waverly Place began airing on Disney, which helped her build a financially stable career.

She appeared in such movies as Breakers and acted as a producer in several projects as she grew up into adulthood. For instance, she is an actress and a producer on the acclaimed Hulu Series Only Murders In The Building on Hulu.

The runaway success of Only Murders In The Building has augmented her finances, and enhanced her reputation in the entertainment sphere by leaps and bounds due to her multifaceted performance and a producing credit.

Producing Credits

Selena Gomez has made significant strides in the entertainment industry as a producer. She has been involved in the production of several successful series, including the critically acclaimed 13 Reasons Why, which delves into sensitive topics like mental health and bullying.

Gomez also produced Living Undocumented, a documentary series that explores the lives of undocumented immigrants in the United States. Her producing efforts earned her a Daytime Emmy nomination for Selena + Chef, a cooking show.

These ventures have diversified her career and contributed to her impressive net worth.

Rare Beauty Business Venture

Selena Gomez established the cosmetics company Rare Beauty in 2019. The company reportedly generated about $60 million in sales in its first year thanks to its rapid rise to fame and online popularity.

Rare Beauty emphasizes self-expression and mental health, while challenging unattainable norms of beauty. The company sells a variety of cosmetics, from base to lip colors, all of which are made to complement different skin tones.

Selena Gomez actively uses the company as a platform to promote mental health through programs, including the Rare Impact Fund, and her participation in it goes beyond business.

Endorsements

Working with major brands such as Coca-Cola and Coach (among others) has dramatically benefited Selena Gomez.

She has a series of endorsement deals that span fashion, beauty, and sports, including those from Louis Vuitton, Pantene, Adidas and Puma. These partnerships raise the brands' profile and increases revenue streams to her financial portfolio, a win for both parties involved.

She has managed to maintain authenticity, while at the same time taking advantage of her global appeal by being very selective about the deals to which she agrees. Such endorsements underscore her sharp yet lucrative approach to capitalizing on her fame.

Investments

Selena Gomez has invested wisely, which has immeasurably amplified her estimated $800 million worth. She has also invested in several consumer brands, like Serendipity Ice Cream and GoPuff.

Additionally, Gomez and Lena Dunne co-founded the mental health startup Wondermind, which managed to secure $100 million worth of capital. All her investments and cooperation in branding have diversified her portfolio.

Mental Health Startup

Wondermind is a mental health firm Selena Gomez co-founded, intending to bring attention to critical problems like addiction and despair. The startup raised $100 million in funding and received considerable media coverage.

Serena Williams, a tennis player, committed $5 million and pledged to actively participate in its creative and brand-promotion endeavors, giving it even more notoriety. Wondermind is a significant contributor to Gomez's multi-million dollar net worth.

Social Media Earnings

She has leveraged her massive followership on social media platforms to turn fans into fortune. High follower counts can come more easily to those in Hollywood, and she currently has 426 million followers on Instagram.

Gomez earns an estimated $1.7 million for each of her sponsored posts, representing another way she benefits from her popularity.
