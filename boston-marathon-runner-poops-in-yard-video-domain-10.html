 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  474 words  |  Kelle Repass

Boston marathon runner poops in yard video Reddit is a viral topic in the news. A runner who has yet to be identified was caught on camera pooping in someone’s yard during the marathon. 

The runner found the yard to be the perfect place for relieving herself as well as for getting some PR.

A Reddit user posted the video on the Boston subreddit page, but the moderators later took it down. However, the world is not going to let something as hilarious as this slide by so quickly. 

Someone else took the video from Reddit before it was taken down and posted it on Twitter. 

Boston marathon runner poops in yard video Reddit is the perfect example of need becoming the mother of invention. 

The homeowner’s front door camera recorded the hilarious scene. The runner enters the yard, goes towards the right-most corner, hoping not to get caught, pulls down her shorts, and relieves herself facing the house door.

Unfortunately for the runner, a spectator who could very well be the homeowner walks in on her while she’s taking a dump in his yard. 

Silence prevails all around; the two exchange glances and the homeowner walks out to give the invader privacy. 

The runner gets back up, pulls up her shorts, and rejoins the marathon as if nothing had happened. 

How did the homeowner react to the video?

Understandably, the homeowner was not happy about the impromptu pit stop of the runner. 

“I have no idea how it can be defended, lol. Thank you for the info. I will be filing a report with the BAA,” the user posted in the comments section of the video. 

The user pointed out that, considering human needs, the marathon organizers had set up plenty of portable toilets all along their street.

With their blatant disregard for others’ property, the runner chose to poop in a yard instead of using the portable toilet just a few minutes away. 

On the other hand, many users came out in the runner’s defense. They said the runner wouldn’t do something as embarrassing as this if they had another option. 

Another user said the runner probably couldn’t control their urge anymore, so they chose to relieve themselves in your yard instead of doing it in front of everyone else on Commonwealth Avenue. 

Unexpectedly, many users came to the runner’s rescue. Some even went as far as bashing the homeowner for causing more embarrassment to the runner. 

Boston marathon runner poops in yard video Reddit shows cruel disregard for someone’s place and privacy.

No matter what the world says, what the runner did was unacceptable. No one can understand the homeowner’s plight, and all those defending the runner should try to place themselves in the homeowner’s shoes for once.
