The Truth About Hilary Duffs Ex-Husband Mike Comrie

While there are many cool facts about Hilary Duff and Matthew Koma, as fans love their romance and enjoy seeing Instagram pictures of Hilary's cute kids, it's easy to forget that Hilary was once married to hockey player Mike Comrie. According to Good Housekeeping, the couple got married in 2010 and Hilary decided that she wanted a divorce in February 2015. There was news that they had broken up in 2014, and fans wondered why they were going their separate ways.

There was some negative press about Mike Comrie a few years back, so let's take a look at what happened.

What Happened With Mike Comrie?

Although fans didn't like a Hilary Duff movie, the actress is praised for starring on Lizzie McGuire, Younger, and many other great movies. After marrying Matthew Koma in December 2019, Hilary's career has been going amazingly well, and her fans are thrilled to see her succeed. Fans feel connected to Hilary since she played Lizzie for so long and anyone who grew up watching that charming show has wanted to continue following Hilary and supporting her.

After news broke that Mike Comrie and Hilary Duff were getting divorced, there was some troubling news about him.

In 2017, the LAPD said that Mike Comrie was investigated for "sexual battery." According to Us Weekly, TMZ reported that Mike and the woman knew each other, and the woman alleged that Mike assaulted her at his condo in Los Angeles.

According to E! News, Mike Comrie wasn't charged with r*pe, and a LA County District Attorney's office spokesperson explained to the publication that that there was "insufficient evidence" so the case was dropped.

The Divorce

The Daily Mail wrote an article saying that Mike Comrie was at a Beverly Hills restaurant called Mastro's Steakhouse and that he had too many drinks and was asking women to sleep with him. TMZ reported that Hilary filed for divorce the day following the publication of that story.

According to People, Hilary Duff suggested that getting married at a young age was what spelled disaster for the couple. Hilary said "I don’t think we were mature enough to stick it out" and explained, “I was 22 when I got married. I’m now divorced, I have a 4-year-old — I wouldn’t change any of it for the world." Hilary is now 34 so it's interesting to hear her reflect on her younger years and fans like hearing her POV on love and marriage.

In a podcast interview with Nico Tortorella, who played Josh on Younger, on his show The Love Bomb, Hilary talked about how she wasn't certain she would get married after divorcing Mike. Hilary also said that she and Mike were still on good terms: "I never want to have any negative — I mean, that’s hard to say no negative feelings — but we got together based on love and we separated in a very loving way. I can’t imagine going through that process with anyone but him and he continues to be my very good friend."

Since Hilary and Mike share their son, Luca, co-parenting is something that comes up a lot.

When Hilary Duff was featured on the cover of Cosmopolitan in 2017, she shared in the interview that she had celebrated Thanksgiving with Mike, and it sounds like they were able to remain close even after splitting up. Hilary explained, “Mike’s amazing. We’re so ingrained in each other’s lives. I wouldn’t choose anyone else to coparent with. We are really good friends and care a lot about each other.”

Hilary Duff has been romantically involved with several stars, but it looks like her love story with Matthew Koma is definitely here to stay. According to Pop Sugar, Hilary previously dated Joel Madden, Frankie Muniz, and Jason Walsh, who was her personal trainer, before falling for Matthew. Now Matthew and Hilary have two daughters, Banks and their new baby Mae, and fans can keep up with the sweet family by following Hilary on Instagram. Hilary often lets fans know what's going on in her life, from sharing that Luca was at camp this summer and she missed him to wishing Matthew a happy Father's Day and praising all that he does for the family. It seems like they are a super tight, sweet family unit.
