 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  157 words  |  Tandra Barner

Busta Rhymes landed in hot water with the law this week. TMZ reports the Brooklyn native allegedly chucked a protein beverage at another attendee at the Steel Gym in New York City (Aug. 5).
