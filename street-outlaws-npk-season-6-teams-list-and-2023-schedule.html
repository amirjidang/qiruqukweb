Street Outlaws NPK Season 6 teams are Team Ryan Martin, Team Kyle Kelly, and 6 others. NPK 2023 is scheduled for four months, June to October.

It’s now a team sport where eight team captains will each draw a crew of drivers who will contest jointly at every NPK event.

Among the nine non-captain, people can choose one captain who has the opportunity to draft his team of racers to compete in NPK.

People voted in May 2023 and chose The People’s Choice Captain as Daddy Dave, among the candidates Kayla Morton, Birdman, Jerry Bird, Jim Howe, Lizzy Musi, Robin Roberts, and Scott Taylor.

Daddy Dave, Ryan Martin, Shawn Ellington, Justin Swanstrom, Mike Murillo, Kye Kelley, Dean Karns, and Jay Boddie are the eight teams captain who selects their four drivers.

All the drivers battle it out for bragging rights and huge cash prizes.

How NPK Season 6 Draft Went Down?

People voted for Daddy Dave, The People’s Choice Captain, who joined the other seven captains.

While determining the NPK team 2023 draft order, Karns picked Jerry Bird at first in NPK Season 6 Draft.

Afterward, the second pick went to Swanstrom, who chose Jim Howe, an NPK two-time NPK event winner.

In the first round, Mike Murillo, Boddie, Ellington, Martin, Dave, and Kye Kelley selected Scott Taylor, Nate Sayler, Jeff Lutz, Robin Roberts, Kayla Morton, and Lizzy Musi, respectively.

Fans believe Swanstrom has the best team while Boddie has the worst.

The audience felt weird Boddie Jr. was not on his father’s team. Nonetheless, they speculated he wanted to be on his own to learn and grow.

They were even surprised Ryan Martin took Robin Roberts as his first pick, which was a bold choice.

For the second round, the draft order got reversed. Boddie picked Mike Bowman, while Karns selected Marty Robertson.

Swanstrom was among the captain who picked Tony McKinney in the final draft.

Some of the racers James Love, Joe Woods, James Goad, Shawn Wilhoit, and David Adkins, were not drafted and put in a reverse pool.

They will get a chance to join the team in different circumstances.

Fans said it seemed humiliating for Reaper, as no captain picked him in the draft. Also, they added they will regret not picking up Doc and Wilhoit.
