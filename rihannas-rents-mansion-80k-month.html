You Can Rent Out Rihanna's Mansion... For $80k A Month

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  434 words  |  Patria Henriques

Those who are interested in living in the lap of luxury, and would like to get a taste of Rihanna's lavish lifestyle can now officially live in her home. That's right - with a few little caveats, of course. Rihanna is worth an astounding $600 million, so she's accustomed to living a certain way. Fans that would love to roam her home now have the opportunity to do so. They can soon say they have officially lived in Rihanna's residence, sat on her furniture, and bathed in her shower. House Beautiful reports that there is now open access for fans to occupy her residence, as long as they are willing to spend $80k a month.

The lifestyle of music mogul and fashionista extraordinaire, Rihanna is definitely far from an ordinary one. She has amassed an astounding following of 102.5 million fans on her Twitter account alone, and she has grown accustomed to a very extravagant way of living.

As she continued to smash records with her music, Rihanna also began to acquire a substantial amount of real estate. One of her many glorious homes is situated on 5,909 squat feet in Beverly Hills, and anyone that is interested, and can afford the hefty price tag can rent it out and live like Rihanna.

The expansive mansion boasts 5 enormous bedrooms and includes a massive four car garage, along with a kitchen that is definitely designed for entertaining. The backyard of this home comes complete with a courtyard that has an enormous fire pit, creating the perfect end to every evening. Of course, there's a huge pool and spa area as well.

There are 5 full baths and 2 partial bathrooms to select from, as well as a massive bar area that was definitely designed to host some of the most elaborate high-end parties known to the area.

Those who are able to afford the rental of this property will also enjoy the opulent neighborhood and high-powered celebrity neighbors that surround this Post Office area estate. This property is close to the mansion that Tyler Perry lent to Meghan Markle and Prince Harry last year, and also near Madonna, and Mariah Carey.

While the monthly rental price seems enormous to most people, the fascination with Rihanna's lifestyle and the ability to actually stay inside her home is going to be tempting to many loyal fans. Those who can afford $80k a month won't want to pass up this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.
