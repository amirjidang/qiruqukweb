What Happened To 'Love Island's' Arabella Chi?

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  574 words  |  Kelle Repass

Arabella Chi, who appeared on the Love Island 2019 edition for only a week, certainly made a mark on the TV series world, not unlike the wealthy Farrah Abraham. So what really happened to the blonde bombshell, and what is she up to now?

What Happened To Arabella On The 'Love Island' 2019?

Arabella is a model from London who describes herself as “energetic” and “fun.” When asked why she thinks she will be a great Islander, she said, “I’m definitely a very energetic person. I bring a fun element, there’s no one in there that is the joker. I like banter. I like playing fun tricks on people. I really want to find someone in there.”

The star also admitted that she is so “ready” to find somebody, noting that she knows what she wants after previous relationships. After arriving in the dating show in week three, she stole Danny Williams from Yewande Biala and had disagreements with some of the women – getting her dumped from the villa.

What Arabella Is Up To Now?

Recently, the model has reportedly had her lip fillers dissolved to achieve her “more natural look.” Apparently, she has followed in the footsteps of world-influencer Kylie Jenner and Molly-Mae Hague after visiting a clinic on Harley Street in London to had her old lip filler replaced with more natural chemicals.

A source told The Sun that Arabella has “been desperate to sort her lips out and get a more natural look,” adding that: “She’s a model so it’s important she looks as real as possible. Arabella is really pleased with the results and relieved as she was a little worried about it.”

Meanwhile, the reality star has revealed earlier this year how she was blacklisted by top fashion designers due to the size of her bum. She admitted in an interview, not unlike other Love Island stars, how she would have to constantly fight for her bum as modeling agencies urged her to lose weight, telling her that she’s “bottom-heavy.”

She said, “I love my bum but I think it is because I’ve learned to love it. It’s what makes me proud. My hip measurement was always a measurement that modeling agencies told me I had to get down. But when they measure your hips, it’s actually your bum. I used to cast for really big shows and for Fashion Week and they would always tell me, if you didn’t have a bum you’d walk for Chanel and Dior…and I’m like, I can’t do anything about it.”

Fortunately, Arabella was using perky bum as an opportunity to tell and encourage people to be fit. She said that it’s been “amazing” to see how cultural perspective has changed and how everyone wants a bigger behind like hers. She also assured fans that she will focus on “body and fitness” and share her secrets on the OnlyFans platform as many are interested in how she keeps her body toned.
