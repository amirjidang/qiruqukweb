Relationships with large age gaps are nothing new in Hollywood. However, there are some stars who have a noticeable tendency to date fellow stars who are significantly younger than they are. Leonardo DiCaprio recently made headlines after he broke up with his model girlfriend Camila Morrone soon after she turned twenty-five years old. Camila and Leo had been dating for several years, and Twitter users even predicted that he would break up with Camila because Leo has never publicly dated anyone above the age of twenty-five. The jokes on social media about Leo's most recent break-up have been ruthless to say the least.

Though the Wolf of Wall Street star's dating history is especially peculiar, he is not the only celebrity in Hollywood who tends to date people who are at least a decade younger. Sometimes, relationships with large age gaps can lead to controversy. Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly; now exes Zach Braff and Florence Pugh; and Aaron and Sam Taylor-Johnson have all faced criticism for the age gaps in their relationships. Continue reading to find out which celebrities have notoriously dated fellow stars who are way younger than they are over the course of their career.

Most recently, Kate dated singer Goody Grace in 2020. Kate is over twenty years older than Matt, Pete, and Goody Grace.

Scott has reportedly dated actress Bella Thorne, influencer Bella Banos, model Megan Blake Irwin, model Sofia Richie, and Lisa Rinna's daughter Amelia Gray.
