14 Celebs Who Love Billie Eilish (6 Who Stay Away)

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  793 words  |  Martina Birk

Billie Eilish is taking over the music industry faster than even she could have ever imagined. The singer first started out when she was only 15 years old, and considering how tough the industry is, she's dealt with it like a champion.

Eilish's songs are currently sitting comfortably in the top 10 spots of every major music chart, which is quite the feat for the now 17-year-old singer. Despite only having three albums, her most recent landing at number one, Eilish has graced fans worldwide with her hit songs and countless fashion looks.

Billie has managed to befriend numerous big names in the business, many of which claim to be huge fans of the singer! From Julia Roberts, Justin Bieber and Ariana Grande, here is a list of 14 celebrities who love Billie Eilish, and a few who aren't as lucky to be friends with her.

39 Loves Her: Charli XCX

38

Charli XCX and Billie Eilish have been friends for quite some time! The two have been spotted hanging out at numerous events throughout the past two years, and have wholeheartedly supported one another's music. When Charli was asked about Billie in an interview, she went as far as comparing her to Kurt Cobain, a huge compliment!

37 Loves Her: Selena Gomez

36

Selena Gomez, who has undoubtedly made her mark in the world of music, is too, a fan of Billie Eilish! The two have met a few times at award shows and events, and Billie's brother, Finneas O'Connell, has even produced a few songs for Gomez, which is how the two first met!

35 Loves Her: Justin Bieber

34

Justin Bieber declared his love for Billie's music when she first broke out on the scene, and it is no surprise that Billie was clearly a fan of Justin's. The two officially met at last year's Coachella, and later worked together on Billie's hit song 'Bad Guy', which reached the number one spot on the H0t 100 Chart.

33 Loves Her: Melanie C

32

Melanie C is also a huge fan of Billie Eilish! It appears meeting Billie was something the singer "really really wanted". The Spice Girl, who Billie has mentioned are huge role models for her, was super happy to meet Billie during a run-in at a radio interview. We love that these two are fans of one another!

31 Loves Her: Lauren Jauregui

30

Lauren Jauregui, who was once upon a time a member of Fifth Harmony, is not only a close friend of Billie's but a huge fan of her music. The singers may have very different styles of music and fashion choices, but it's clear that they support one another and have been able to create a special bond.

29 Loves Her: Camila Cabello

28

Speaking of Fifth Harmony, Camila Cabello, who was also part of the girl group, has expressed her massive adoration for the 17-year-old singer. The two have met several times, and even shared a moment on one another's Instagram Stories earlier this year. We love seeing women support women, and these two are doing just that.

27 Loves Her: Julia Roberts

26

Julia Roberts has declared she is "obsessed" with Billie Eilish! This pairing is definitely not one that we would ever expect in a million years, however, we are so happy it's happened! Roberts and Eilish have met on numerous occasions, and believe it or not, Julia is the one who always asks to meet her! The actress is in love with the singers' music and has made it very clear.

25 Loves Her: Ariana Grande

24

The current pop princess Ariana Grande is sharing the love with newcomer Billie Eilish! Although Ariana only scored her first-ever number one hit on the Billboard Hot 100 last August, she's still giving Billie as much advice as possible. The industry is by no means easy, and Ariana knows it, so it's nice to see her guiding Billie down the right path.

23 Loves Her: Post Malone

22

Not this is definitely a pair we could have totally imagined happening. Post Malone and Billie Eilish both broke out on the scene around the same time, and it was always love and no competition between the two. They both share similar styles in music and fashion and are not busy getting caught up in fame, but just enjoying life and riding the music wave.

21 Loves Her: Melissa McCarthy

20

As if Julia Roberts wasn't one actress too many, Melissa McCarthy has expressed her ever-growing love for the singer. Melissa went on the 'Ellen Show' last year to declare her adoration for Billie Eilish, where she was then surprised by Billie herself! It was the meet and greet none of us knew we needed.
