The Cast Of Amazon Prime's The Summer I Turned Pretty Isn't As Rich As You'd Think

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1121 words  |  Patria Henriques

The Summer I Turned Pretty was a huge success on Amazon Prime last summer. The coming-of-age romantic drama included a cast of new and familiar faces with exceptional talent. It explores family, friendship, first love, heartbreak, and grief.

The show scored major points by playing Taylor Swift's music throughout the episodes.

The concept for the show was taken from the corresponding book. But fans are curious to know how wealthy this amazing cast is. Let's dive in.

11 Lola Tung Is Worth $250,000 - $450,000

Tung plays Belly. She goes to Cousins with her family every summer and hangs out with Conrad and Jeremiah. As Belly prepares to go to Cousins for another summer, it is revealed that she has a crush on Conrad. Belly finds herself in a love triangle between the two brothers who were once her friends. She also experiences her first heartbreak.

According to Insider, this is Tung's first professional acting role, and her audition process was through Zoom. "The whole process happened over Zoom and was really amazing and wonderful. Every chemistry read was over Zoom. Everything. I feel very, very grateful," she said.

When she isn't acting, she's focused on her studies. Tung is a student at Carnegie Mellon University. She currently has a net worth of $250-450k (approximately).

10 Gavin Casalegno Is Worth $1 Million

Gavin Casalegno plays Jeremiah. Jeremiah is popular and always down for a good time. He has his vulnerable moments and a crush on Belly.

According to Radio Times, Casalegno has appeared in the TV series Walker and The Vampire Diaries. He was also featured in the film Noah.

Casalengo is worth $1 Million, as reported by Next Biography.

9 Christopher Briney Is Worth $1.5 Million

Christopher Briney plays Jeremiah's older brother, Conrad. Conrad is Belly's crush. He is mostly standoffish and moody. His family and close friends realize that his personality has changed.

After learning about his mother's cancer diagnosis, he keeps the information to himself. This puts him in a bad space all summer. He finally gets back to his true self in the last episode when he apologizes to Belly for his actions and confesses his feelings for her.

According to PopSugar, Briney graduated from Pace University. He worked in theater before joining the series.

As reported by The Wiki Feed, Briney's net worth is $1.5 Million.

8 Sean Kaufman Is Worth $1 Million

Sean Kaufman plays Belly's older brother, Steven. He is also best friends with Jeremiah and Conrad. Steven works at the Country Club to earn money.

He falls head over heels for Shayla. Their relationship is tested throughout the episodes, but they come to an understanding.

According to Romance Devoured, he's had roles in FBI: Most Wanted and Law & Order: SVU. Kaufman also had a recurring guest role on Manifest.

As reported by Gossip Gist, Kaufman is worth $1 Million

7 Jackie Chung Is Worth $2 Million

Jackie Chung plays Laurel. Laurel is Belly and Steven's mother. She and Susannah are best friends. For both Conrad and Jeremiah, she is almost like a mother to them.

According to Collider, Chung has been seen in Station 19, Someone Else, and Grey's Anatomy.

Chung is worth $2 Million, as reported by Popular Bio.

6 Rachel Blanchard Is Worth $2 Million

Rachel Blanchard plays Susannah. Susannah is Conrad and Jeremiah's mother. She and Laurel are best friend. Susannah is also a mother figure to Belly and Steven. She advised Belly to take on the role of debutante.

Sadly, Susannah is battling cancer but keeps it a secret from those around her. She wants to have a perfect summer. Eventually, the truth comes out. Susannah decided she wouldn't continue with her treatments. Eventually, her sons urged her to, and she goes along with the idea.

According to The Sun, she was best known for starring as Cher Horowitz in the Clueless TV sitcom.

She recently appeared alongside Ben Affleck in the Hulu drama Deep Water (2022).

Blanchard is worth $2 Million, as reported by Spice Cinemas.

5 David Iacono Is Worth $2 Million

David Iacono plays Cam aka Cam Cameron. He sees Belly and remembers her from eighth grade. They meet again at a beach bonfire and spark a connection. Cam and Belly begin dating. Unfortunately, they break up when Belly learns she has feeling for someone else - Conrad.

According to The Wrap, Iacono has appeared in TV series like The Flight Attendant and Grand Army.

Iacono is worth $2 Million, as reported by Married Biography.

4 Rain Spencer Is Worth $1.5 Million

Rain Spencer plays Taylor. She is Belly's best friend. Taylor is outgoing and fierce. She is the first and only person that knows about Belly's crush on Conrad.

When Belly catches her kissing Steven (Belly's brother), things almost turn sour between the two friends. Belly is furious. Steven, who is already in a relationship with Shayla, asks Belly to keep it between them. Taylor admits to Belly it won't happen again.

According to Today, Spencer recently starred in the film "Good Girl Jane," which stars Andie MacDowell. Spencer made a recurring appearance in the TV series The Super Man (2017).

Spencer is worth $1.5 Million, as reported by Popular Networth.

3 Minnie Mills Is Worth Around $500,000

Minnie Mills plays Shayla. However, Mills' character did not appear in The Summer I Turned Pretty books but was written in the show. Shayla is Mills' first acting role.

Her character is a debutante dating Steven, Belly's brother. She is one of the popular girls at Cousins. Shayla wears nice outfits and has rich friends.

According to Grand Peoples, Mills has a net worth of $500K.

2 Alfredo Narciso Is Worth $100,000 - $1 Million

Alfredo Narciso plays Cleveland, an bestselling author in Cousins writing his next book. He gets close to Laurel and has Conrad teach him how to sail.

According to Marie Claire, Alfredo Narciso has had recurring roles in popular shows like Manifest, Home, Marvels Jessica Jones, and House of Cards.

As reported by Idol Networth, Narciso is worth $100K- $1 Million.

1 Summer Madison Is Worth $300,000

Summer Madison plays Nicole. Nicole's romance with Conrad plays out through the summer until they break up. Nicole is also a debutant. She is friends with Shayla and later befriends Belly.

According to L'officiel, her acting credits include The Wonder Years, Crazy Neighborhood Moms, First Wives Clues, and much more.

Madison is worth 300K, as reported by Celebs Fortune.
