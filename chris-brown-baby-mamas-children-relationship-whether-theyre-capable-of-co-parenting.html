Chris Browns Relationship With His Three Baby Mamas Is Completely Different

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1430 words  |  Tandra Barner

Highlights

  • Chris Brown is the proud father of three children by three different mothers, and despite their different relationships, they are all putting the children first.
  • Brown's co-parenting relationships have improved over time, and he is now actively involved in his children's lives, being a present father.
  • Becoming a father has changed Brown's priorities, and he has matured and shifted his focus to his family, rarely partying or going out anymore.

Chris Brown is celebrated for his music. While he has enjoyed tremendous success with his songs and collaborations with artists like Chloe Bailey, not to mention a resurgence in popularity as a result of TikTok, his love life has been, perhaps, less successful. Brown has had multiple high-profile relationships, and some of them have produced children.

He is now the proud father to three children with three baby mamas. The relationships with these women are all incredibly different. Yet somehow, it appears they are all making it work by putting the children first.

Update August 2023: While Chris Brown does what he can these days to co-parent peacefully with all of his mothers of his babies, it was not always the easiest to have a good relationship with these women. Over time, however, Brown and the women decided to put the best interests of the children first and put any petty arguments behind them.

Though Brown has a past that is marred with domestic violence and anger management issues, there do not appear to be any current legal issues pending between himself, Nia Guzman, Ammika Harris, or Diamond Brown. In fact, the women clearly feel confident in Brown's ability to parent, given that in April 2023, he posted a picture where he was posing with all three of his children in his home. As such, the siblings are getting to know one another versus being estranged and Brown is being a present father.

Whether any of these co-parenting relationships will ever turn into something romantic is anyone's guess. Currently, Brown is not dating any of the women who had his children and is single.

Who Are Chris Brown's Children And Who Are Their Mothers?

Brown became a father for the first time when Nia Guzman gave birth to their daughter, Royalty, on ​​May 27, 2014. In the beginning, their co-parenting relationship was rocky, and the pair were involved in court battles over the custody of their daughter. Brown also temporarily stopped paying child support after allegedly being denied visitation of Royalty in 2015.

Since that time, their relationship has significantly improved, and they now have a great co-parenting relationship.

"So I have a co-parenting job that's pretty amazing but, you know, learning how to be a dad, especially learning how to take care of somebody else, you know, I barely know how to brush my teeth in the morning!" Brown explained. "It's actually great. It's very humbling. You know, it's very calming. I think I was a lot, you know, rambunctious, very hyper as a kid, but now seeing that ten times over, my daughter's kinda like mellowing me out."

He would become a father a second time with model Ammika Harris. The pair had a son, Aeko Brown, who looks identical to Brown, born on Nov. 20, 2019. Co-parenting with Harris would be a little more challenging because of their different geographical locations. She is based in Germany and chose to quarantine there during the pandemic. However, the pair managed to find a way to work around this with the help of technology.

Then in 2022, he welcomed his third child with Diamond Brown, who Brown was known to date on-and-off in the three years leading up to Diamond getting pregnant. The couple had a daughter, Lovely Symphani, born on Jan. 7, 2022. Reports suggest that Brown has been an involved dad and that he and Diamond live close enough to one another that he can see Lovely often.

"Chris and Diamond have known each other for years and dated casually for a while," a source explained. "Chris and Diamond started off as friends, and she and her girlfriends would travel with Chris while he was touring and on the road."

The source went on to say, "That allowed them to become much closer over time, and even though he has dated Diamond off and on, he's not looking to settle down with anybody right now."

What Has Chris Brown Said About Being A Father?

Brown is perhaps better known for his legal drama, including his alleged assault on ex-girlfriend Rihanna in 2009 than he is for being a dad. But becoming a father has changed him in many ways, which some believe has been for the better.

After the birth of his second child, it was reported by a source that Brown was "head over heels in love with Aeko and can't believe he's so blessed to have two incredible children." They also revealed that he had made family his priority.

"He rarely goes out or parties anymore, and the only time he's away from [his kids] is if he's working," the source said. "His priorities have completely shifted, and he's matured so much over these past few years since becoming a father."

"The best part of being a dad, you know, doing those little things," Brown explained to E! News. "Bedtime is crazy for me 'cause I always was like... trying to avoid bedtime when I was a kid, like, 'No, Mom, just 15 more minutes, please!' So I'll keep like the kids' channel on and turn it all the way low and turn all the lights out, and you know, I'll lay in the bed with her and like rub her back until she actually falls asleep. But some nights, she's not having it. Like, she just is totally energetic..."

As positive as Brown is about his children, the same can be said about what he says about the mothers of his children as well.

What Has Chris Brown Said About The Mothers Of His Children?

Given Brown's past known anger management issues, it can be hard to imagine that he manages to parent three children and keep a good relationship with the women who gave birth to his kids. If reports are to be believed, however, then despite each of his relationships with his baby mamas being very different, Brown has figured out a formula that works.

Guzman is the first woman to make Brown a father with the birth of their daughter, Royalty. While the duo initially had their problems after Royalty was born, they have since buried the hatchet and have figured out how to co-parent peacefully.

"Chris gets along great with Nia because they decided a long time ago to make things work for the sake of Royalty," a source explained to HollywoodLife.

"For Chris, Royalty is the most important girl in his life, and he is willing to do whatever it takes to show her how much her parents love her and that she sees how well they get along."

When Brown's son, Aeko, was born, he found a way to be involved in his life, even from a distance.

"Chris and Ammika are co-parenting really well. They stay in touch all the time and are constantly FaceTiming, so Chris is able to keep up with Aeko as he's growing," a source told HollywoodLife. "He doesn't want to miss any little milestones since Aeko is growing so fast. Ammika has been really good about sending a ton of pictures and videos to him all the time."

The musician's life has also changed significantly since becoming a father to Aeko.

"He's learning so much," the source went on to say. "He sacrifices everything and anything to be there taking care of Aeko, and Ammika, whenever possible. Aeko has been such a blessing to his life, and Chris can't wait to watch him grow up."

Last, Brown's relationship with Diamond Brown and how they parent their daughter, Lovely. A source stated, "He's played a part in her life since before she was born."

There are reports that Brown has bought lavish gifts for Diamond, including a luxury car. Brown wants both his child and the mother of his child to be comfortable and safe driving and it appears he is willing to spend the big bucks to make this happen.
