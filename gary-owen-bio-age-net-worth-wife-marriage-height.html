Career

Gary Owen is both a professional actor and a stand-up comic. As a stand-up comedian, he was dubbed “America’s Funniest Serviceman.” Following his honor, the comedian received his big break in 1997. It was during the stand-up showcase Comic View on Black Entertainment Television.

Following this success, the comedian appeared as a guest star in a number of films, including College, Daddy Day Care, Rebound, Hillbilly Highway, and Little Man. Additionally, the comedian had a recurring role on the show House of Payne as Zack. Additionally, he played Larry in the comedy Meet the Blacks.

Similarly, the comedian/actor toured the All-Star Comedy Jam Tour with Shaquille O’Neal. As a result, the comedian has amassed a sizable following among African-Americans.

Additionally, this comedian won a contest for “Funniest Black Comedian in San Diego.” Following his victory, the comedian landed his first gig at Hollywood’s The Comedy Store. The actor/comedian then won his own show, The Gary Owen Show, in 2016.

Prior to his successful career as a comedian, he served six years in the United States Navy. In the Navy, he held the rank of Master-at-Arms. Similarly, during his time in the Navy, he served on the Presidential Honor Guard.
