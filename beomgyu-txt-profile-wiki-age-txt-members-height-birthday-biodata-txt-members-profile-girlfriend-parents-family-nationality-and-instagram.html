Beomgyu TXT Profile – Beomgyu Wiki

Beomgyu born Choi Beom Gyu, is a member of the boy band, TXT. TXT stands for “Tomorrow X Together,” pronounced as the Korean translation “Tomorrow By Together”.

Beomgyu was revealed as the fifth member of Big Hit Entertainment’s boy band TXT on January 20, 2019. Other TXT Members Yeonjun was introduced on January 10, 2019, Soobin was revealed on January 14, 2019, Huening Kai was revealed on January 15, 2019 and Beomgyu on January 17, 2019.

#TXT (#투모로우바이투게더) – #범규 #BEOMGYU #TOMORROW_X_TOGETHER pic.twitter.com/1BEz8GxiYf

— BigHit Entertainment (@BigHitEnt) January 20, 2019

In an introduction video, Beomgyu is seen goofing around on a train, taking selfies and generally enjoying himself. The video ends with a blank screen as Morse code is heard, which fans quickly decoded to translate into the word “Dreaming.”

Beomgyu TXT Age

He was born on March 13, 2001 in Daegu, South Korea.

Beomgyu Nationality

He is of Korean nationality.

Beomgyu Height

He stands at a height of 5 feet 10 inches (178 cm).

TXT Debut

TXT made their official debut on March 4, 2019. TXT members released their debut EP, The Dream Chapter: STAR, along with their first music video for “Crown,” on March 4, 2019.
