Astead Herndon Biography and Wiki

Astead Herndon is an American journalist and reporter who is known for his political coverage throughout Washington DC. Herndon is known for having worked for The Boston Globe and The New York Times, he is also known for his work as a political analyst on CNN. He was born on March 31, 1992, in Brooklyn, New York, the United States as Astead Wesley Herndon. Herndon attended Marquette University where he earned his Bachelor’s degree in Journalism and Political Science.

He started his career as a news reporter in the year 2009 for The Homewood. After working with the Marquette Tribune and several other papers, Herndon became a Featured Reporting Intern for CNN in May of 2014.

While attending Marquette University, Herndon worked as a McBeth Fellow which allowed him to contribute to several longer articles for the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel.
