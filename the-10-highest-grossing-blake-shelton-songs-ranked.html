Howdy there, fellow country music enthusiasts! Let's take a leisurely stroll down memory lane and relive the sweet sounds of one of Nashville's most celebrated talents; Blake Shelton. From tear-jerking ballads to boot-stomping anthems, this chart-topping cowboy has serenaded us with some of the most unforgettable tunes in recent history. His unique style and undeniable charm have won him countless fans across the globe. Over two decades of hits to his name, his music has become a mainstay on country radio and beyond. From the heartwarming God's Country to the classic Austin, these songs have captured the hearts of fans young and old. So, grab your cowboy hat, turn up the volume, and join us on this unforgettable journey through the best of Blake Shelton.

Came Here to Forget - 2016 (Gold)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Blake Shelton dropped a soul-stirring ballad with Came Here to Forget in 2016, expertly produced by none other than Scott Hendricks. This slow-burning tune showed off Blake's impressive vocal range and emotional depth, hitting listeners right in the feels. The song's popularity didn't take long to catch fire, earning itself a shiny Gold certification from the RIAA. Came Here to Forget is the kind of song that'll have you reaching for the tissues and feeling all the feels, thanks to Blake's raw talent.

Over - 2012 (Gold)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Blake Shelton gifted us with the soulful country ballad Over in 2012. This song was a pure showcase of Blake's vocal talents, as he poured his heart out with those gut-wrenching lyrics and pulled at our heartstrings. It's no wonder this song became a fan favorite and went on to earn a shiny Gold certification from the RIAA. Over is the kind of tune that'll make you want to grab a whiskey and reminisce about the one that got away or the times that were tough.

Goodbye Time - 2005 (Gold)

Via Flickr/Discogs

Produced by the legendary Brent Rowan and released in 2005, Goodbye Time showcases Blake Shelton's vocal range and emotional depth in a way that'll leave you breathless. The melancholic lyrics and heartfelt delivery make this song a pure masterpiece that hits you right in the feels. Earning a Gold certification from the RIAA, this is the kind of song you listen to when you're going through a tough breakup, or when you're feeling nostalgic for a love that's long gone.

Some Beach - 2004 (Gold)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Back in 2004, Blake Shelton released the feel-good hit Some Beach, produced by Bobby Braddock. This song was a reflection of Blake's playful side, as he serenaded fans with those catchy melodies and hilarious lyrics that had everyone chuckling. This is the kind of song that makes you want to kick off your boots and head down to the nearest sandy shore or just soak up the sun and forget about your worries and its gold certification is evidence of that.

I'll Name the Dogs - 2017 (Platinum)

Via Flickr/Country Thang Daily

In 2017, Blake Shelton dropped a real heart-warmer with I'll Name the Dogs, with some help from the legendary producer Scott Hendricks. This tune had all the ingredients for a hit, with its swoon-worthy lyrics and catchy melody capturing the hearts of listeners far and wide. The song's popularity soared to new heights, earning itself a shiny Platinum certification from the RIAA, and it's easy to see why.

Sangria - 2015 (2x Platinum)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Blake Shelton graced us with the sultry and oh-so-romantic Sangria in 2015, produced by Scott Hendricks. This tune had all the hallmarks of a classic hit, with its smooth melody and steamy lyrics leaving listeners weak in their knees. It's the kind of tune that'll make you want to grab a glass of your favorite red and get lost in the moment. It is no surprise Sangria garnered a 2x Platinum certification from the RIAA. Cheers to that!

Mine Would Be You - 2013 (2x Platinum)

Via Flickr/Taste Of Country

Looks like we have ourselves a heart-tugger here! Mine Would Be You, released in 2013 and produced by the mastermind Scott Hendricks, quickly became one of Blake Shelton's most beloved songs and it's not hard to see why; those romantic lyrics, that emotional delivery, and that voice! Oh, that voice! It's one of the numerous mind-blowing factors that earned this song a 2x Platinum certification from the RIAA. This tune is a pure masterpiece that'll have you feeling all the feels.

Boys 'Round Here - 2013 (2x Platinum)

Via Wikimedia Commons

If there's one thing Blake Shelton knows how to do, it's crank out hit after hit, and Boys 'Round Here is definitely no exception. This jam, produced by the talented Scott Hendricks and released back in 2013, had fans hooked with its infectious chorus and catchy melody. The song's popularity soared to new heights, earning itself a 2x Platinum certification from the RIAA, and it's easy to see why it remains a beloved fan favorite to this day.

Honey Bee - 2011 (3x Platinum)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Blake Shelton blessed us all with the irresistible jam that is Honey Bee in 2011, thanks to the magic touch of producer Scott Hendricks. This tune's upbeat tempo, catchy lyrics, and the playful vibe had fans swarming to it like, well, bees to honey! It's no surprise that the song's popularity buzzed so high that it garnered a 3x Platinum certification from the RIAA. Being one of Blake's most adored hits, it's easy to see why it's still a crowd-pleaser to this day.

God's Country - 2019 (5x Platinum)

Via Flickr/Country Music Nation

Blake Shelton dropped a real banger with God's Country, expertly produced by Scott Hendricks in 2019. This tune didn't just hit, it exploded onto the scene, instantly capturing the hearts of country music fans everywhere. With lyrics that pack a punch and a melody that pulls you in, it's no wonder this ode to rural living earned itself a shiny 5x Platinum certification from the RIAA. God's Country is one of Blake's biggest and most beloved hits to date.
