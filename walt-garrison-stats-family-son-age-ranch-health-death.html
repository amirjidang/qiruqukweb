Walter Benton Garrison, known for his remarkable career as a fullback in the National Football League with the Dallas Cowboys, was not just a football icon but a man of many dimensions. His journey, marked by impressive stats on the field and a love for rodeo, family, and ranch life, is a story worth exploring. In this article, we delve into the life of Walt Garrison, his family, his passion for rodeo, and the extraordinary legacy he leaves behind.

Walt Garrison: The Football Stats

Born on July 23, 1944, in Denton, Texas, Walt Garrison’s football career is punctuated by impressive statistics. He rushed for 3,886 yards, averaging 4.3 yards per carry, and scored 30 touchdowns during his time with the Dallas Cowboys. His contributions on the field earned him a FantPt (Fantasy Points) total of 776.0, a testament to his skill and dedication.

Family: A Key Part of His Life

Family played an essential role in Walt Garrison’s life. He was married to Deborah Johnston from August 23, 1986, until February 1, 2005. Before this, he was married to Pamela Phillips from June 30, 1967, to August 30, 1985. His family was a source of support and love throughout his life.

His Son’s Perspective

Walt Garrison’s son, Marty Garrison, shared insights into his father’s life. He revealed that Walt’s first love was rodeo, a passion that took root from a young age. This love for rodeo continued to burn brightly alongside his successful football career.

From Ranch to Legacy

After his remarkable career, Walt Garrison’s former ranch transformed. It now stands as an extraordinary 26-acre equestrian estate, once home to the Southern Methodist University (SMU) equestrian team. This transformation serves as a testament to the enduring legacy of a man who cherished his ranch life.

Walt Garrison’s Passing

On October 11, 2023, the sports world mourned the passing of Walt Garrison. While the cause of his death remains undisclosed, his legacy lives on in the hearts of those who admired him both as a football legend and as a man with a passion for rodeo, family, and ranch life.

An Enduring Legacy

Walt Garrison’s life was a remarkable journey filled with achievements, passions, and love for his family. His football stats are just one facet of his legacy, and his impact on the rodeo world, his family, and his ranch life continues to inspire. As we remember the legend, his legacy lives on in the stories and memories shared by those who knew and admired him. Walt Garrison’s life will continue to be celebrated for generations to come.
