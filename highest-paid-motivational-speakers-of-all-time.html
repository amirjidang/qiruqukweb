Becoming successful in life can be a challenge. We all look to each other for the answers or some kind of direction along our journey. Hearing words of wisdom sometimes motivates us to get back in the game and give it our all.

There is a powerful impact that motivated and steadfast people can instill in us. Becoming inspired by their stories and their life journeys helps us put one foot in front of the other. These people lift us up and keep us moving forward. Let's take a minute to rise up and thank the following 10 highest paid motivational speakers of all time...

Joe Rogan

Joe Rogan is a man who does it all. He is a certified black belt in jiu-jitsu, he has his own podcast and is an advocate for a healthy lifestyle. Millions tune in on a regular basis to listen to what Joe has to say.

He welcomes guests from all walks of life onto his show and is very open-minded. He is confident in his opinions and is always ready to learn something new. Rogan currently has a net worth of $25 million and his bank balance continues to grow. He seems to get wiser with age and has a huge, diverse following.

Joyce Meyer

Sometimes the most successful motivational speakers are ones that have overcome a difficult past. Joyce is a perfect example of this. She suffered abuse from her father growing up and is one strong woman.

She was able to find God and that helped carry her through some of the hardest times. She preaches the Word of the Lord around the world and has amassed a net worth of $8 million. She has set up ministries that allow her to speak her truth through podcasts, television, conferences and special events. She has also helped many underprivileged children throughout the world with her many charities.

Tony Robbins

Tony Robbins is one of the most well known motivational speakers of our time. He considers himself as a life coach and has helped millions through his power of positivity. He has written many self-help books. The most popular one is Awaken The Giant Within. This book has sold millions of copies worldwide and has changed many lives.

Robbins uses strategies and techniques for people to use in order to better their lives. He works closely with celebrities and is widely recognized in Hollywood. His net worth is roughly $500 million so he is definitely doing something right!

Joel Osteen

Joel Osteen is one of the most popular American pastors today. He is based out of Houston, Texas and is head of Lakewood Church. His sermons are televised worldwide and have a massive following of over 7 million viewers per week. He grew up in a religious household and was immersed in the preaching world by his father.

He welcomes everyone into his church and preaches a message of living a good life. He currently has a net worth of roughly $100 million dollars! His followers are inspired by him and his message, he impacts many people positively on a regular basis.

Iyanla Vanzant

You may remember the name Iyanla Vanzant because of the oh so popular queen herself, Oprah! Oprah introduced Iyanla to the public and many people are very grateful that she did. Iyanla is unafraid to speak the truth and let people know what they need to hear. Her show, Iyanla: Fix My Life, focuses each episode on helping an individual overcome tribulations within their lives.

People can relate to what others are going through so the show is very successful. Iyanla has a net worth of roughly $4 million and it continues to grow each day. She has the boldness that influences people to get up and change their lives.

Daymond John

You may recognize Daymond John from the popular television show Shark Tank, but is much more than a judge on this show. He created a hugely successful clothing business called FUBU. In doing so, he has made himself over $250 million! He is a no-nonsense type of man and will do whatever it takes to remain successful.

He inspires people who want to make their own business succeed with his career story. He also gives back to the community by offering training programs. The programs help teach people about all the ins and outs of the entrepreneur world. His leadership and generosity will help guide future business people to success.

Eric Thomas

It is amazing to hear the life stories of some of the most successful motivational speakers of all time. Eric came from being homeless on the streets for two years to amassing a net worth of over $2.5 million! His story inspires people to never give up no matter how bad their life seems to have gotten.

He dedicates his life to giving back and preaching powerful messages. He inspires underprivileged kids to go in the right direction and has set up programs to assist them. Life can be difficult and Eric has overcome some of the most difficult tribulations. He definitely has a positive message to give!

Suze Orman

Suze Orman specializes in educating people about financial well being. She is a one-woman army with a lot of power, focussing on the important role that money plays in our lives. She leads conferences nationwide and inspires people on a regular basis to better their finances. She's also the author of nine New York Times best-selling books!

She currently has her own podcast and has won two Emmy Awards throughout her career thus far. For someone with so much knowledge about money, we are dying to know how much she has! Orman sits comfortably on a net worth of $50 million!

Bif Naked

Bif Naked, also known as Beth Torbert, is a musician and inspiring woman. After a successful music career, Beth now focuses on speaking engagements and encouraging people, especially women.

She was diagnosed with breast cancer at 41 years old. Her talks focus on the struggles of cancer and how it can affect your life. She stresses the importance of patient well being. She also focuses on healthy body image and women's empowerment. She has come a long way from rocking out on stage. and is able to inspire others and give them strength in times when they need it most.

Magic Johnson

Magic Johnson is a name that most people have heard out at some point in their lives. He is best known for being a huge basketball star in the NBA. He shocked the world in 1991 when he announced that he was infected with HIV.

He has dedicated his life to battling this scary illness. He has created the Magic Johnson Foundation in order to help fight HIV and was the keynote speaker for the United Nations on World Aids Day. Johnson has created an open dialogue about HIV and has educated the public on the effects of having it.
