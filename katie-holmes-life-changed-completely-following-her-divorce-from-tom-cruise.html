Katie Holmes' Life Changed Completely Following Her Divorce From Tom Cruise

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  807 words  |  Patria Henriques

Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes got married in 2006. At that point, it appeared as though the Hollywood A-list couple was in it for the long run. However, less than a decade later in 2012, the couple dropped on bombshell on Hollywood, deciding to go their separate ways. Although details were kept very quiet, it is believed that Tom Cruise was stunned by the decision.

In the following, we're going to take a look at how Katie Holmes' life changed drastically following the split. Katie Holmes left the Hollywood scene with daughter Suri, and agreed to live a completely different life in New York. We'll reveal the changes that Holmes made, and how she was able to cope with all the headlines at the time. We're also going to reveal how much Tom and Katie sold their former Beverly Hills home for.

Katie Holmes Left Beverly Hills For A Life In The City Following Her Split From Tom Cruise

Following Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes' divorce, the couple made major changes. Among them, was selling their Beverly Hills home. According to Daily Mail, the stunning property sold for $40 million, which was $10 million under the asking price.

The publication writes, "Tom Cruise has sold the Los Angeles mansion he shared with ex-wife Katie Holmes. The star offloaded his seven-bedroom, 10,000 square foot mansion in Beverly Hills for $40 million,TMZ reports. The buyer was billionaire Leon Black, the founder of private equity group Apollo Global Management, the gossip site reports."

Katie Holmes proceeded to make a major change. Although she didn't leave the acting scene behind, she did make a move to New York City, far away from her former home. It was one of the many major changes made by Holmes following her split from Tom Cruise.

Katie Holmes Revealed That Her Life Completely Changed When She Made The Move To New York With Suri

Katie Holmes hasn't said much when it comes to her relationship with Tom Cruise, likely because she signed an NDA. However, she did open up about how the story impacted her life, and how she finally was able to find peace.

"Obviously, as a public person, I've gotten a lot of attention at different periods in my life. And when you have a lot of attention, sometimes you don't want to leave the house because it's just too much."

She continued, "You can get consumed by what people think, but suddenly you just decide to do things on your own terms. I feel like I'm finally figuring that out. I'm getting the projects I want made and just relaxing a little. I've been around for a long time, and I'm still here! I can't believe it."

Holmes would also discuss how different the experience was living in New York, but how those around her made the transition an easy, and positive one.

“Yes, it's a little too exposed at times,” she admitted to InStyle. “We work hard to maneuver and navigate. But what I love about New York is that for me and my child, this is our vibe.”

"New York has been very good to both of us. I love the theater, the galleries in my neighborhood. Stuff [like] that is so much of what makes me love this city. I love the Knicks. I love talking to my neighbors in the elevator. I like feeling a part of something big."

Holmes would go on to compliment all the things she has to her disposal. “When there are 25 things to do every night, it takes you out of your own thing… I'll take a class at Broadway Dance Center. I'll go to the Joyce Theater. I do hot yoga and boxing classes. I have a book club. The city has a lot to offer, and I use it.”

A major lifestyle change and according to tabloids, she was desperate for it.

Rumors Suggest Katie Holmes Agreed To Ridiculous Term In Order To Get Out Of Her Marriage With Tom Cruise

Both sides kept things quiet when it comes to the details about their split. However, there are several rumors circulating, some state that Holmes was forced into signing a ridiculous deal, just so she could leave the relationship.

She’s allowed to date, but she cannot do so in a public fashion, and she’s not supposed to let any boyfriend near their daughter, Suri.”

We'll never really know for sure what went on behind the scenes.
