Does C2H2Cl2 have a double bond?

With C2H2Cl2 there are only single bonds. Carbon is the least electonegative atom so it goes at the center of the C2H2Cl2 Lewis structure. Remember that Hydrogen (H) atoms always go on the outside of a Lewis Structure. Note that Hydrogen only needs two valence electrons to have a full outer shell.

What is the hybridization of carbon in C2H2Cl2?

How does the hybridization of carbon change as it goes from C2H2 + Cl2 -> C2H2Cl2 ? The hybridization of carbon changes from sp to sp3. There are 3 bonds in C2H2 molecule, however there are only two bonds connected to the central atom, C, therefore the hybridization would be sp.
