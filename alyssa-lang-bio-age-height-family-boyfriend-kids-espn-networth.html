Alyssa Lang ESPN

Lang is a host, anchor, and reporter for SEC Network who joined the network in the summer of 2018. She later signed a new deal in the summer of 2019. She has two known shows, Out of Pocket with Alyssa Lang and Thinking Out Loud with Alyssa Lang. Lang works as a college football and basketball sideline reporter for the SEC Network. Additionally, she is also one of the anchors of SEC Now. She shares the anchor desk with network regulars Peter Burns and Dari Nowkhah on a rotating basis.

Moreover, during the NFL season, she co-hosts ESPN Radio’s Primetime with NFL insider Field Yates on Sundays. Lang worked at First Coast News in Jacksonville for two seasons before joining the ESPN and SEC Network families. She also worked at WLTX in Columbia, South Carolina. She covered South Carolina, Clemson, the Carolina Panthers, and other topics until moving to Florida. Lang has also worked for SPEED TV, GamecockCentral.com, and Charlotte-based WCNC.
