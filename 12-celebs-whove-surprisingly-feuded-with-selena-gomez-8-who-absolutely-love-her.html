12 Celebs Whove Surprisingly Feuded With Selena Gomez (8 Who Absolutely Love Her)

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1603 words  |  Patria Henriques

Selena Gomez has enjoyed a very successful career in Hollywood, having landed her first big break on Barney & Friends when she was just 10 years old. Since her TV debut in 2002, the Texas-born beauty has not only launched a booming acting career but also a music platform. Her music career has really skyrocketed and she's sold over 20 million records worldwide.

It goes without saying that Selena has proven herself to be quite a force to be reckoned with. But despite all the success and fame, she's had a lot of low moments -- most of which stemmed from her feuds with several of her Hollywood peers. While Selena has rarely ever spoken badly about others, these 12 celebs didn't have the best encounter with the "Hands To Myself" singer, or so it appears.

But with every foe comes a friend - here are 12 celebs who've surprisingly feuded with Selena Gomez (and eight who absolutely love her).

20 Miley Cyrus - Feud

In 2014, in the midst of her "Bangerz" world tour, Miley Cyrus entertained fans as she grabbed a cardboard cutout of Selena Gomez while singing her song, "FU", in Milan, Italy, while audience members enjoyed the snark.

Miley and Selena have a history together; they both feuded over the same guy - Nick Jonas. He dated both of them during his teenage years, and his romance with Selena was longer-lasting than his fling with Miley.

19 Chris Rock - Feud

In 2016, Chris Rock made some not-so-nice comments about Selena. He rudely described her as a poor man's version of Beyoncé. This totally uncalled-for comment got the comedian into a lot of trouble, as Selena's fans lashed out at Chris for daring to draw comparisons between the two Hollywood stars. Gomez never responded to what was supposed to be an innocent joke.

18 Ariana Grande - Feud

While this is more of a rumor, it's believed that Selena and Ariana Grande aren't exactly friends. Fans are well aware of Ariana's close ties with Miley Cyrus. Given that the "Wrecking Ball" hitmaker isn't fond of her frenemy, Selena, could that potentially mean that Ariana isn't welcoming the idea of being friends with Selena, either? Ari has never made it a secret that she always has her friend's back.

17 Stefano Gabbana - Feud

In 2018, Stefano Gabbana left Selena's fans in an uproar when he deemed the former Disney Channel star, "ugly." The co-founder of the Dolce & Gabbana fashion brand came under fire on social media after making the comment, with thousands of fans hitting back at the designer and his bullying attitude towards a woman. On top of that, Stefano never apologized for his remarks.

16 Demi Lovato - Feud

While Demi Lovato and Selena were once close pals, their friendship has had its ups and downs -- at this point, it honestly seems as if the two have gone their separate ways for good.

In 2014, during an appearance on Watch What Happens Live, the show's host, Andy Cohen, asked why Demi had unfollowed Selena on Instagram, to which she responded, "I think it's one of those things where people changed, and people grow apart."

15 Justin Prentice - Feud

13 Reasons Why star, Justin Prentice, found himself in a lot of trouble after appearing to have made several comments targeting Selena Gomez in May of 2017. From his official Instagram account, Justin apparently said, "So glad you produce our show #thosewhocantactproduce #knowyoplacebooboo," leaving fans completely stunned. He later claimed he was hacked, but do you believe it?

14 Hailey Baldwin - Feud

Hailey Baldwin Bieber and Selena Gomez have been feuding, it seems. But it's probably because of their fans attacking one another on social media. Since Justin Bieber went on to marry Hailey, just months after splitting from Gomez, Selena's fans aimed their insults at Hailey, creating a false narrative that's now developed into a feud between the two ladies.

13 Madison Beer - Feud

Madison Beer found herself in hot water when she dined with Justin's wife, Hailey Baldwin Bieber, out in Los Angeles, while supposedly having shared a close friendship with Selena in the past.

While it's unclear whether Madison and Selena are still cool with one another, fans didn't like the fact that the singer was spending time with Hailey one bit, calling her a "traitor."

12 Lorde - Feud

Lorde didn't receive quite the reaction she'd hoped for as a feminist, after dissing Selena's song "Come & Get It" by shaming it for its raunchy lyrics. Taylor Swift's bestie wasn't impressed with the choice of words used in the song, insisting: "I'm a feminist and the theme of her song is, ‘'When you're ready, come and get it from me,' I'm sick of women being portrayed this way."

11 Bella Hadid - Feud

When Selena Gomez started seeing R&B singer, The Weeknd, Bella Hadid was livid because she apparently had plans to eventually reconcile with her on-off boyfriend. He'd taken a break from Bella to focus on his career. It apparently resulted in a lot of tension between her and Selena, given that she was in the way of Bella Hadid getting back with her ex.

10 The Weeknd - Feud

Soon after his split from Selena, The Weeknd was featured on Nicki Minaj's song, "Thought I Knew You," which many believe featured lyrics that were aimed at his ex-girlfriend. The Weeknd sings about a complicated relationship, adding that the person he was seeing isn't who he thought she was. The release of the song, so close to when Selena and her former beau had just called it quits, is interesting.

9 Jake Paul - Feud

After cutting ties with Disney and abruptly leaving his show, Bizaardvark, in the middle of its series, Jake Paul threw shade at Selena Gomez as he tried to draw comparisons between himself and Selena Gomez.

Jake wasn't happy with Disney, so he quit. And while he got a lot of backlash for it, he argued that people like Selena can do it without the same response.

8 Jennifer Aniston - Friend

In what seems to be quite the surprise friendship, Selena and Jennifer Aniston have been close buddies for years. In fact, it's even believed that the former Friends star helped her pal stay on the path of sobriety. Apparently, Gomez reaches out to Jennifer whenever she feels the need to gain clarity and advice from someone with wisdom and knowledge.

7 Taylor Swift - Friend

Perhaps her closest friendship in Hollywood is with Taylor Swift. The two met while they were both still dating two of the Jonas brothers - Joe and Nick - and have remained on good terms ever since. From sleepovers to supporting one another with their music, these girls do it all together, and it's apparent that Selena cherishes their friendship.

6 Niall Horan - Friend

Niall Horan, who was once said to have been crushing on Selena Gomez, has been a loyal friend and supporter of the singer for as long as fans can remember. Even when he was still part of One Direction, Niall had nothing but positive things to say about Selena and any encounters they had in the past. He's even plugged her music on his social media pages.

5 Taylor Lautner - Friend

Taylor Lautner famously dated Selena in the early years of their careers, and though their romance didn't work out, they've remained friends ever since. The two have shared a close bond and Taylor has supposedly always been a shoulder for Selena to cry on when things became too much for her. It's nice to stay friends with an ex, that's for sure.

4 Cardi B - Friend

Selena became friends with Cardi B following their collaboration on DJ Snake's "Taki Taki" in 2018. The song was a smash hit, but it was the bond she built with Cardi that's sure to last a lifetime.

Speaking of working with Selena, Cardi previously dished: "When I met her, she was such a sweet, adorable person. She's really what you see. She's really a sweetheart."

3 Amy Schumer - Friend

Just months after professing her love for Amy Schumer, Selena made her debut on the comedienne's comedy sketch show, Inside Amy Schumer, playing a lead singer in one of the segments. But apparently, their get-together only came about after the blonde heard about how much of a fan Selena was -- and the two are said to have been friends ever since.

2 Gigi Hadid - Friend

Though there was said to have been a short-lived feud between Selena and Bella Hadid over The Weeknd, Gigi Hadid apparently never involved herself in that situation. She's been friends with Selena for years, thanks to her own ties to Taylor Swift and her circle of pals. Selena and Gigi have even traveled the world together and are still said to be close.

1 Nick Jonas - Friend

Again, it's good to stay on good terms with an ex. Selena Gomez definitely knows a thing or two about that since she's also said to be friendly with Nick Jonas, who has since married Indian actress, Priyanka Chopra. Though Selena and her ex don't see each other often, it's said that there's nothing but love between the two...and that's how it's supposed to be.
