Agnes Mooreheads Former Beverly Hills Home Sold For $28 Million

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  434 words  |  Tandra Barner

American actress Agnes Moorehead.. In a career spanning four decades, her credits included work in radio, stage, film, and television.

Though Modi Wiczyk already owns a $7.5 million mansion on the same desirable Beverly Hills street, just a couple of blocks away, the Tinseltown stalwart has quietly forked over millions more for a Mediterranean-style villa that was occupied by “Bewitched” star Agnes Moorehead during the 1950s — and before her, by legendary Golden Age actor Spencer Tracy.

Records reveal Wiczyk — a Harvard MBA who’s churned out the likes of “Ozark,” “House of Cards,” “Baby Driver,” “Knives Out” and “Ted” as co-founder and CEO of independent studio MRC Entertainment — and his wife Kate Phillips (the daughter of late movie producer Julia Phillips) purchased the property in an off-market deal for a speck under $29 million. That’s a whopping $12.8 million more than Dan Ashoori, a real estate developer who owns several IHOP franchises, paid for the place back in early 2017. But the almost century-old mansion has been extensively renovated and modernized since then.

Because the new Wiczyk residence was never publicly offered for sale, current photos and insider details are scarce. But previous listings show that the two-story structure offers eight bedrooms and seven baths spread across nearly 12,000 square feet — a significant size adjustment from the couple’s other Beverly Hills home, which has roughly 7,200 square feet of living space.

Tucked away behind gates on almost three-quarters of an acre, the Mediterranean-style estate is fronted by a circular driveway and manicured lawn dotted with palm trees. Once inside the double hard-carved front doors, there are soaring ceilings and pegged wood floors throughout, according to Dirt.

Among the highlights: a double-height foyer that steps up to a living room boasting a vaulted wood-beam ceiling, limestone fireplace and five sets of French doors spilling outdoors. A formal dining room rests beneath a coffered ceiling sporting hand-painted murals, the kitchen has updated appliances and a breakfast nook, and a fireside master retreat comes complete with a pair of private balconies overlooking the pool, as well as dual dressing rooms and baths.

Elsewhere in the house are a wine room and gym, along with a wet bar-equipped media and billiards room. Outdoors, the lush grounds host a spacious courtyard with fountains that’s ideal for al fresco entertaining and lighted tennis court, plus a pool flanked by a cabana with its own sitting room and full bath.
