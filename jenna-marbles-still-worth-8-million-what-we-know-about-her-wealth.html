Is Jenna Marbles Still Worth $8 Million? What We Know About Her Wealth

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1089 words  |  Tandra Barner

If you watch videos on YouTube, it is very unlikely that you haven't heard of Jenna Marbles. Although her real name is in fact Jenna Nicole Mourey, this 33-year-old YouTuber has an emblematic persona on the video platform.

Jenna entered the world of YouTube videos in 2010, back when there wasn't such a thing as being an 'Instagram influencer' or even a 'Vlogger'. As she uploaded her first video, under a pseudonym she made up based on the name of her famous Chihuahua, "Mr. Marbles", Jenna went viral. This was thanks to her video, "How To Trick People Into Thinking You're Good Looking".

The video made YouTube history after it was viewed over 5.3 million times in its first week. What happened next is history.

As one of the most established YouTubers in the world, Jenna's net worth is estimated to be $8 million. In this article, we take a look at everything we know about her wealth.

As some articles by industry insiders reveal that advertised videos usually make between $0.10 to $0.30 per view, this would mean that a YouTube channel would typically receive $18 per 1,000 ad views. Although the math may seem a little confusing, we can estimate Jenna Marbles' videos to earn her approximately $6000, as she always has between 2 to 4 million views per uploaded video.

13 She Receives Money For Attending Events

Once a person has officially reached celebrity status, they can earn money from basically anything. This includes attending events. 12 Her Yearly Income Was Over $300,000 in 2013

Back in 2013, an article published in Business Insider stated that the YouTuber's yearly income was $346,000. During that year, Jenna had less than 15 million subscribers, and a little less than 1 billion views in total. Now, the 33-year-old YouTube star has over 19 million subscribers and nearly 3 billion views. Therefore, we can only guess that Jenna's yearly income has doubled, if not tripled.

11 She Also Makes Money From Her Twitch Channel

In addition to her very popular YouTube channel, which has been running for a decade now, Jenna shares a Twitch channel with her partner, Julien. Their video gaming platform has over 230,00 followers and helps the couple to add additional income. Ads are playing simultaneously videos while viewers are watching.

10 Jenna Gets Money From Her Sponsored Podcast

The 33-year-old YouTube star works on many projects. Indeed, Jenna also makes an income from a podcast she runs with her boyfriend. Jenna and Julien discuss fan fiction, conspiracy theories, personal stories, and more. Jenna and Julien's podcast is also sponsored, which allows the couple to receive a steady stream of money.

9 She Earned Money From Acting

The YouTube star hasn't limited herself to her channel. As a matter of fact, Jenna has acted on other platforms. Maybe she'll end up doing more acting gigs in the future.

It is unclear whether "Miss Marbles" will continue her radio work; however, she did host a weekly radio segment for many years. Jenna Mourey, A.K.A Jenna Marbles, has also stepped into behind-the-camera work for a film. That's right - in 2016, Jenna worked as an executive producer for a movie called Maximum Ride. The movie features the actress, Allie Marie Evans, the actor Gavin Lewis, and other talented cast members.

Subscribers and views aren't the only things that matter to a YouTuber. Jenna's longtime dedication and passion, which lead her to create memorable video content, have also led her to be awarded multiple times.
