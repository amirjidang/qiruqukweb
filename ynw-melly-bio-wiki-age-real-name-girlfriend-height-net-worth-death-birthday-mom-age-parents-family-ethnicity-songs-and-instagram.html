YNW Melly Biography – YNW Melly Wiki

YNW Melly real name Jamell Maurice Demons, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter.

He started posting his songs on SoundCloud when he was 15. In late 2017, Demons released his first project, an EP called Collect Call. In 2018, he released the singles “Virtual (Blue Balenciagas)”, “Melly the Menace”, “Slang That Iron”, “4 Real”, “Butter Pecan”, and “Medium Fries”.

In August 2018, Demons released his debut album I Am You. That same year Melly signed with Young Thug’s YSL Records. On January 17, 2019, Demons released We All Shine, his second commercial mixtape.
