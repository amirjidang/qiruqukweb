Here's What These Celebs Had To Say About Kevin Spacey And His 'Creepy' Behavior

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  944 words  |  Tandra Barner

Kevin Spacey was once an actor who could demand any salary and was sure to stun fans with one epic performance after another on the big screen. His career was peppered with wildly successful films such as American Beauty,The Usual Suspects, and House Of Cards, to name a few. At the height of his career, he was a well respected actor, and was one of Hollywood's favorite, most familiar faces.

Sadly, his entire reputation took a huge hit when a number of accusers came forward with allegations of sexual assault and misconduct. Since then, his career took an absolute nose-dive, and his friends, fans, and fellow celebrities turned against him, with complete disdain over his actions. Some celebrities have spoken out against the former star, depicting their rage over his creepy behavior.

8 Alyssa Milano

After Anthony Rapp accused Kevin Spacey of inappropriate sexual advances when he was juts 14-years-old, Kevin Spacey released a highly controversial video response that set fans and colleagues into a flurry of concern. The video was a clear depiction of his lack of empathy in them matter, and Alyssa Milano was among the first stars to speak out. She urged fans to let his behavior speak for itself and called his responses "disturbing" and accused him of chastising his audience. Her response garnered a lot of support from fellow stars.

7 Patricia Arquette

Former star of Medium, Patricia Arquette, spoke out both against Kevin Spacey and in support of his victims. She slammed him for his inappropriate video and said that his victims surely didn't appreciate or need to be exposed to further toxicity.

Newsday reported on the heavy criticism that came his way after being accused of making advances towards Rapp in 1986 were not something to deny or make light of, and Arquette expressed disgust for this display of poor behavior and Spacey's clear lack of remorse.

6 Wanda Sykes

Wanda Sykes took aim at Spacey over his remarks about being gay and using his sexual orientation as a defense to "explain away" his behavior toward Rapp. The fact that Spacey said he "did not remember the encounter" that so clearly traumatized Rapp and have affected his whole life, really set off Wanda Sykes. She blasted Spacey for trying to hide behind his own terrible deeds and dragged him for misrepresenting the LGBTQ community. Sykes refused to accept this explanation as being acceptable, and pushed back on his association with the gay community.

5 Richard Lawson

Richard Lawson, best known for his incredible acting in the hit film Poltergeist, showed some empathy to the fact that it seemed Kevin Spacey, himself, was facing a personal struggle in his life. However, he is not willing to let go of the fact that no matter what personal issues Spacey was facing at the time, even if they were conflicting issues with his own sexuality, the fact remains that Rapp was just 14-years-old. Lawson makes it clear that 14-years-old is an age of innocence, and there is absolutely nothing to excuse Spacey's behavior towards that child.

4 Mena Suvari

Mena Suvari opened up about a creepy encounter she had with Kevin Spacey on the set of American Beauty. She revealed that during the filming of this epic film, she was preparing to shoot an intimate scene with Spacey and indicated that she trusted him at the time. However, she tells Indie Wire she remembers getting what she describes as a "weird feeling" after he lay on the bed very close to her, after taking her into a side room. She described the moment as being "peaceful but weird and unusual" and now says she looks back at it differently after learning about his sexually deviant practices.

3 Billy Eichner

Billy's impeccable skills as a talk show host has made him a fan-favorite for fans across the globe. When he spoke out against Kevin Spacey, his fans were definitely tuned in for the update. Billy sent a sarcastic message that was written with precision and clearly put Spacey's actions and responses in the spotlight. By writing; "Kevin Spacey has just invented something that has never existed before, a bad time to come out," he was declaring that even the gay community was not able to stand behind him in support. This was a clear misrepresentation of homosexuality, and Billy called Spacey out, point blank. Spacey had pushed this matter way too far.

2 Ellen Barkin

Ellen Barkin holds a long history as an actress and producer and is behind hugely successful films such as Johnny Handsome and Sea Of Love. She drew on Kevin Spacey's work and life in a parallel fashion and accused him of chastising his audience. She rang in with the same message that others had for Spacey, which was that his messaging and responses to the allegations are disturbing and completely unacceptable to all involved.

1 Paula Pell

Paula Pell is best known for her time on Saturday Night Live, and she decided to keep the comedic commentary flowing with more than just a tinge of sarcasm. She mocked Kevin Spacey's futile efforts to hide behind his conflicted sexual identity and made it clear to him that he would not be embraced by the LGBTQ community. After trying to use his orientation to justify his malicious actions, he is not welcome nor will he be embraced by those who proudly declare themselves to be homosexual.
