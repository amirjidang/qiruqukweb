How old is Natasha Leggero? Get to know the host of Rat In The Kitchen and her family in the article below. 

Natasha Leggero must not be a new name for most. But, if you don’t know who she is, let me tell you.

Natasha is an American comedian, actress, and writer from Silverlake, Los Angeles. She rose to fame after hosting the MTV reality television series The 70s House in 2005.

Further, her iconic hosting of Chelsea Handler’s late-night talk show Chelsea Lately from 2008 to 2014 is also something she is known for.

And now, she is presenting the kitchen show called Rat In the Kitchen, a show where the chefs cook dishes to impress the hosts, but someone among them will be sabotaging the food.

It sounds pretty hilarious. But let’s see how it turns out on Television as it is premiering on March 31.

Following the trailer and all the buzz regarding the show, Natasha is getting more and more attention. And now, fans are curious to know about her particulars, her daughter, husband, and family.

So, go through it all in the writing below.

Rat In The Kitchen: Natasha Leggero Baby Daughter

Natasha Leggero is the mother of her beautiful daughter, whose name is yet to come out as neither of her parents has spoken about it.

She first revealed her pregnancy on October 3, 2017, during her interview with Stephen Colbert. And the following year, on February 24, 2018, the comedian took over her Instagram to announce the birth of their daughter.

You can spot the daughter’s picture here and there on her social media.

To Whom Is She Married? Natasha Leggero Husband Moshe Kasher And Family

Natasha is married to the American stand-up comedian, writer, and actor Moshe Kasher.

After dating briefly, they turned their bachelor romance into an inevitable bond in 2015 and are raising their 4-year-old daughter in Los Angeles.

So, until now, the family comprises only three members, but let’s see if the couple wants to expand it more in the approaching days.

How Old Is The Rat In The Kitchen Host?-Natasha Leggero Age & Birthday

Natasha Leggero was born on 26 March 1974 in Rockford, Illinois, carrying Italian and Swedish descent.

Despite being raised as a Roman Catholic, the 48-years-old converted to Judaism as an adult.

On the other hand, her husband was born in Queens, New York, on July 6, 1979, making himself 42-years-old in 2022.
