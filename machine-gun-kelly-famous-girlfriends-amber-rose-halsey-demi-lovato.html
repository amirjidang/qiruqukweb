All 10 Of Machine Gun Kelly's Famous Girlfriends, In Chronological Order

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  933 words  |  Billy Koelling

Musical artist Machine Gun Kelly's dating history includes some of Hollywood's most beautiful women. As one of the top talents in the music industry right now, it's not shocking that he's been linked to so many gorgeous ladies including Halsey, Chantel Jeffries, and most recently, actress Megan Fox.

MGK seems to be getting serious with the Transformers star. From their undeniable connection on his "Bloody Valentine" video, where the two get pretty steamy, to cute Instagram photos of the pair sticking their tongues out, it appears that the new couple is settling down. However, let's look back at MGK, whose real name is Colson Baker, and his past relationships with some of the top celebs in Hollywood.

10 Emma Canon

Machine Gun Kelly welcomed his daughter Casie in July 2008, but little is known about Casie's mother, Emma Cannon. Since the birth of their daughter, the couple parted ways, and it appears that Canon has no social media or photos online.

However, MGK loves taking his now 12-year-old daughter along with him to red carpet events like the Nickelodeon Kid's Choice Awards, and fans can usually see photos of the pair together on his Instagram.

9 Rachel Starr

Machine Gun Kelly is known for his wild behavior, so it wasn't surprising when the rapper began dating adult film star, Rachel Starr.

The pair appeared to be together in 2013 and did some things in a Dallas nightclub that are just too explicit to comment on. According to the Dallas Observer, the two had a "friends with extreme benefits" relationship.

8 Amber Rose

Amber Rose and Machine Gun Kelly struck up a romance in April 2015, but it only lasted a couple of months with MGK revealing to Hot 97 that there was too much media attention and spotlight on the pair, which is something Amber Rose is very much fond of.

It was odd to see the two together, since MGK is very blunt on not liking fame and the pressures of it but admitted that it was their busy schedules that led to their split.

7 Halsey

Rumors that singer Halsey and MGK were a new item began in March 2017 when they were spotted together at the beach, and the rapper later sharing a photo on social media of them on vacation in Mexico. The two met on the set of Roadies, but according to MGK, the romance did not last long.

Machine Gun Kelly later stated in an interview that his romance with Halsey made him "feel 16 again," but neither MGK nor Halsey ever really confirmed their relationship.

6 Chantel Jeffries

Model Chantel Jeffries and Machine Gun Kelly were seen hanging out together in Los Angeles on multiple occasions during the summer of 2019, prompting rumors that they were in a serious relationship.

The two were very secretive about their romance, but in September 2019, the DJ expressed her love for MGK by posting an Instagram story of a paparazzi shot of the pair with the words "My Baby," and the rapper later reposted the same photo. However, it's not clear when they split, but MGK did move on to someone new the next year.

5 Kate Beckinsale

Actress Kate Beckinsale was spotted with Machine Gun Kelly in early January 2020 at the Golden Globes afterparty, sparking rumors that the two were an item. The pair have a 17 year age difference, but that did not stop them from dating.

What made this relationship odd was the fact that the actress previously dated Kelly's good friend, comedian Pete Davidson. However, this rumored relationship ended before it began, with MGK linking up with Noah Cyrus in late January of this year.

4 Noah Cyrus

Miley Cyrus' younger sister Noah Cyrus was spotted getting very cozy with Machine Gun Kelly in late January 2020 at a Grammy after-party.

Of course, this sparked major dating rumors between the two artists. Kelly did attend Noah's 20th birthday party that same month, but neither Cyrus nor Kelly confirmed their relationship.

3 Demi Lovato

Fans were freaking out when photos emerge of Demi Lovato and Machine Gun Kelly stepping out of a club together in the wee hours of the morning back in February.

The two were photographed together outside of the Soho House West Hollywood at 2 a.m. However, TMZ reported that there wasn't a romance brewing between the two stars and just hung out as "mutual friends."

2 Sommer Ray

Social media star Sommer Ray and MGK began a relationship in March of this year when the rapper reposted photos of the two together on Twitter.

While the pair appeared to be having a great time together, MGK revealed that Ray broke up with him the following month. He wrote on Twitter, "she came and picked all her stuff up on my birthday. Nice."

1 Megan Fox

MGK's new romance with actress Megan Fox was a shock to everyone since Fox was married to Brian Austin Green for 10 years. Green revealed that the two had separated back and December, and even mentioned Fox's relationship with Kelly stating, "I don't want people to think her or he are villains or that I was a victim in any way."

Since the split, Fox and MGK have not been hiding their romance at all, posting Instagram photos together and sharing kissing with one another in LA.
